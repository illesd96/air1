# Hooks for A 33 Sperber

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Only three of these airliners were ever built — each one custom-ordered for a job that no longer exists.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1930, German aviation had a tier that no longer exists: air taxis. Short, premium, low-volume routes between German cities. Focke-Wulf built exactly three A 33 Sperbers — one for each operator who wanted in. Then Lufthansa standardized the German fleet, swallowed every competitor, and the air-taxi era ended forever. One Sperber briefly limped into Deutsche Luft Hansa as a second-hand bushplane in 1937. That was the end of the entire category.
