# Hooks for Airbus A220

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Boeing tried to kill it with a trade complaint, so Airbus bought it for one dollar.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 2017, Bombardier was building a clean-sheet, geared-turbofan narrowbody that scared Boeing so badly they filed a 300% tariff complaint with the US Commerce Department. The Canadians were broke. So Airbus walked in, took 50.01% of the CSeries program for a single dollar, renamed it the A220, and turned Boeing's trade war into one of the most lopsided heists in aviation history.
