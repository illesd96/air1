# Hooks for Airbus A300

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Born after the French rejected Concorde — and it became the plane that built Airbus.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1969, France's aviation ministry was so embarrassed by Concorde's cost overruns that they bet on the opposite extreme: a fuel-efficient twin-engine widebody. American airlines laughed. Then the oil crisis hit in 1973, fuel prices tripled, and the A300 — the world's first twin-aisle twinjet — became the proof-of-concept that made Airbus the rival Boeing never wanted.
