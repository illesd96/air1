# Hooks for Airbus A320

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Airbus replaced the yoke with a sidestick and put a computer between pilots and the airplane — and changed flying forever.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1988, the A320 entered service with two things no airliner had before: a sidestick control instead of a yoke, and a full fly-by-wire flight envelope protection system. Pilots from the 707 generation hated it. The 737 community mocked it. Thirty-eight years later, fly-by-wire is the standard, the A320 family is the best-selling airliner in history, and Boeing is still playing catch-up.
