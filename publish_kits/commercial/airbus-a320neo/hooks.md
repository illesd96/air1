# Hooks for Airbus A320neo

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The A320neo quietly beat the 737 MAX — and after the groundings, Boeing executives privately said the race was over.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

December 2014. Airbus launches the A320neo with two competing engines on the wing: a Pratt & Whitney geared turbofan and a CFM LEAP. Boeing later locks the MAX into one engine only. Then 2019 happens. The MAX is grounded. Net orders flip permanently. By 2023, Pratt's powder-metal scandal grounds hundreds of neos — but the lead is so large that internally, Boeing has already accepted defeat in the single-aisle race.
