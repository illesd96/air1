# Hooks for Airbus A330

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Airbus's least exciting widebody is also the one that quietly outsold everyone — including Boeing.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Launched in 1987 alongside the four-engine A340, the A330 was supposed to be the boring twin. Then the A340 died, ETOPS opened transoceanic routes, and the A330 just kept selling. Over 1,500 delivered, the A330neo is still rolling out of Toulouse in 2026, and a 39-year-old design is still beating Boeing's 767. This is the quietest success story in widebody history.
