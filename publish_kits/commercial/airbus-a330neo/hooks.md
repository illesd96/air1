# Hooks for Airbus A330neo

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Airbus's answer to the Dreamliner was just a re-engined A330 — and it nearly didn't work.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Faced with Boeing's 787 stealing every wide-body order, Airbus chose the cheap path: bolt new Rolls-Royce Trent 7000 engines onto the 1990s A330 airframe and call it the A330neo. Sales were brutal for years. Then COVID flipped long-haul economics, and suddenly airlines wanted a cheaper, slightly less efficient widebody — and TAP Portugal's launch order suddenly looked clever.
