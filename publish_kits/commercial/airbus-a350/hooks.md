# Hooks for Airbus A350

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Airbus's first answer to the 787 was so bad airlines literally laughed at it — so they threw it out and started over.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 2004, Airbus pitched an A330 with new engines as their 787 competitor. Singapore Airlines, ILFC, and Emirates basically said no. Embarrassed, Airbus burned the proposal, spent two years redesigning from scratch, and produced the A350 XWB — a 53% carbon-fiber clean-sheet that's now eating into 777 sales and outranging the Dreamliner.
