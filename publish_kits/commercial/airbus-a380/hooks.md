# Hooks for Airbus A380

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

They built the biggest passenger jet in history, then quietly killed it after just 251 aircraft.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 2005, a 560-tonne whale lifted off from Toulouse and the world believed the future of air travel had arrived on two decks. Sixteen years later, Airbus shut down the line. This is the story of how the A380 won the engineering battle, lost the economic war, and accidentally proved that the hub-and-spoke model was already dying.
