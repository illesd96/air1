# Hooks for Antonov An-124 Ruslan

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Soviet answer to the C-5 Galaxy — and the West rented them when its own planes weren't big enough.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Designed in the 1980s to move Soviet ICBMs and tanks, the An-124 Ruslan ended the Cold War unemployed. So Antonov's Ukrainian and Russian operators turned the world's heaviest production cargo aircraft into commercial freighters — and watched NATO governments rent them to ship their own military equipment. Then 2022 happened, and the fleet split overnight.
