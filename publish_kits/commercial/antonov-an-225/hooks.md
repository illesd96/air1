# Hooks for Antonov An-225 Mriya

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

There was only ever one. The Russians burned it on the ground at Hostomel in February 2022.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Mriya means dream. The An-225 was built to piggyback the Soviet space shuttle Buran, used the engines of six fighter jets, had 32 wheels, and weighed 640 tonnes. Only one ever flew. On February 27, 2022, Russian artillery destroyed it in its hangar at Hostomel, and the largest airplane ever built became the first airliner casualty of a major war.
