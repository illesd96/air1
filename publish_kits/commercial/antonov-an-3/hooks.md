# Hooks for Antonov An-3

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Soviets bolted a turboprop onto a biplane — and made the world's weirdest cargo plane.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Antonov An-3 is what happens when Soviet engineers refuse to let the An-2 biplane die. They yanked the 1947 radial engine, dropped in a turboprop, and ended up with a turbine-powered biplane that can short-take-off from snow, drop firefighters, dust crops, and haul a ton of freight into places no other turboprop dares land.
