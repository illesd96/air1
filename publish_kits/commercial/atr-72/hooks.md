# Hooks for ATR 72

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The plane that quietly carries you to every regional airport you've never heard of.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When you fly to a small Italian island, a remote Indian airport, or a regional hub no jet can serve, you're probably on an ATR 72. Born from a 1981 Franco-Italian joint venture between Aerospatiale and Aeritalia, this 72-seat turboprop sips 45% less fuel than a comparable regional jet, takes off from 1,200-meter strips, and has quietly become the world's best-selling regional airliner of the 2010s.
