# Hooks for British Aerospace 146

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

It has four engines on a regional jet and pilots called it the Whisperjet — so quiet it was the only airliner allowed to fly into London City after dark.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Four small turbofans on a high wing. No leading-edge slats. Massive flaps and brakes. The British Aerospace 146 was the quietest jet airliner ever certified, and it could land on grass strips, mountain runways, and 4,000-foot city airports that no Boeing or Airbus could touch. Aspen, London City, La Paz — the BAe 146 went where nothing else dared. So why did Britain build only 387 of them?
