# Hooks for Boeing 707

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Boeing's test pilot barrel-rolled the prototype over crowds in 1955 — and Pan Am bought it the next day.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

On August 7, 1955, Boeing test pilot Tex Johnston rolled the 367-80 prototype 360 degrees over Lake Washington in front of every airline executive in the world. Pan Am's Juan Trippe ordered 20 of them. The jet age started that afternoon. The 707 didn't just replace propliners — it made London-New York a single shift, killed ocean liners, and built modern Boeing.
