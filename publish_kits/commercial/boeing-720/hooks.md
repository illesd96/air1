# Hooks for Boeing 720

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Boeing built a shorter, faster 707 — and killed it themselves in under a decade.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

It looked like a 707 and flew like a 707, but the Boeing 720 was Boeing's quietest commercial failure. Conceived for shorter runways and medium-range routes, it was outsold by its own bigger sibling, eclipsed by the 727, and gone from passenger service before most travelers knew it existed. Only 154 were ever built.
