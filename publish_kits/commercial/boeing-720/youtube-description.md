The Boeing 720 is the jet Boeing would rather you not remember. Launched in 1959 as a shorter, lighter 707 for medium-range routes, it entered service with United Airlines and... was eclipsed within years by the 727. Only 154 were built, and most were quietly retired or sent to test programs.
