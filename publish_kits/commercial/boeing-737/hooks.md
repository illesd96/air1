# Hooks for Boeing 737

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

There's a 737 taking off somewhere in the world every 1.5 seconds — and it's also Boeing's biggest crisis.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The 737 was supposed to be a stopgap. A small, simple twinjet to fill in the gaps the 707 and 727 couldn't. Sixty years and 12,000 aircraft later, it became the most-produced jetliner in history — and then MCAS, two crashes, and a 20-month grounding nearly destroyed Boeing. This is how the Baby Boeing became both a triumph and a cautionary tale.
