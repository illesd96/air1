# Hooks for Boeing 747

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Pan Am ordered it before the factory even existed — and that gamble made the Queen of the Skies.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1965, Joe Sutter had 28 months and no factory to build the largest airplane ever attempted. Pan Am's Juan Trippe wanted a jet two and a half times bigger than the 707, and Boeing was about to bet the company on it. Fifty-five years later, the last 747 rolled out of Everett — and an entire era of intercontinental travel rolled out with it.
