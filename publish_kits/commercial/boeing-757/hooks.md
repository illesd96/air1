# Hooks for Boeing 757

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Pilots will tell you it's the best-flying airliner ever built — and Boeing killed it anyway.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The 757 climbs like a fighter, lands at hot-and-high airports nothing else can, and pilots near-universally call it the best-handling airliner ever made. Boeing built 1,049 of them, then stopped production in 2004 and refused to bring it back. Twenty years later, every airline still operating one keeps the airframes alive because nothing has replaced what the 757 could uniquely do.
