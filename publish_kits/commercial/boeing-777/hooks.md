# Hooks for Boeing 777

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Two engines, 351,000 lbs of thrust each, and the plane that quietly killed every four-engine widebody.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When the 777 first flew in 1994, the idea of crossing oceans on two engines still made airline executives nervous. Two decades later, the 747, the A340, and the A380 were dead, and the Triple Seven was the most-built widebody in history. This is how ETOPS, GE90 thrust, and an obsessive design process by airline customers ended the four-engine era.
