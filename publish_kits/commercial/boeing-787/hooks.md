# Hooks for Boeing 787

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Boeing built the first plastic airliner — then the lithium batteries caught fire and the entire global fleet was grounded for three months.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

January 2013. Two Boeing 787s in two weeks. One smoldering on the tarmac in Boston, another forced down in Japan with smoke in the cockpit. For the first time since the Comet, regulators grounded an entire airliner type worldwide. Boeing had bet the company on a carbon-fiber, lithium-powered Dreamliner — and for 123 days, it looked like the bet had failed.
