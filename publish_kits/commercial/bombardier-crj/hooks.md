# Hooks for Bombardier CRJ

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

This little Canadian jet invented the modern regional airline — and made it possible for you to fly from Dayton to Dallas without a propeller.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1991 Bombardier took a business jet, stretched it twice, and accidentally invented the regional aviation industry. The CRJ let major airlines abandon turboprops, build out hub-and-spoke networks, and serve hundreds of small cities that big jets couldn't reach economically. Two thousand were built. Half of US domestic flights still feel its influence. So why does almost nobody know its name?
