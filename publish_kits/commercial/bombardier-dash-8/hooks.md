# Hooks for Bombardier Dash 8

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Canadian engineers fixed the vibration problem on the Q400 with active noise cancellation — turning the loudest turboprop into one of the quietest.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Dash 8 was already a Canadian turboprop legend by 1995. Then Bombardier stretched it into the Q400, added 360-knot props, and watched passengers complain about cabin noise so bad that pilots called it the Vibrator. So engineers installed Active Noise and Vibration Suppression — speakers in the cabin actively cancelling propeller harmonics in real time. The Q400 became the quietest turboprop in the sky.
