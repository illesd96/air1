# Hooks for Breda Ba.44

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Mussolini's Italy built a biplane airliner in 1934 — and pressed it into wartime service when WWII began.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Breda Ba.44 was an Italian biplane airliner of the mid-1930s, produced in small numbers for Ala Littoria. When Italy entered WWII, the Regia Aeronautica simply impressed these civilian airliners into military transport service — a fate that befell most pre-war European commercial aircraft. Almost nothing of the Ba.44 survives today.
