The Breda Ba.44 was an Italian biplane airliner developed in the mid-1930s for Ala Littoria, Italy's pre-war national airline. Only a small number were built, and when Italy entered WWII, the Regia Aeronautica impressed them into military transport service — a typical fate for European pre-war civil aircraft.
