# Hooks for British Aerospace Jetstream 31

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Britain's tiny pressurized twin that punched far above its weight on rough US regional routes.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1982, British Aerospace resurrected a failed Handley Page design — the original Jetstream — and turned it into the Jetstream 31. A small, pressurized, 19-seat twin turboprop that quietly conquered the American Eagle and Trans States commuter networks before competition from larger jets pushed it out. It's now nearly extinct in scheduled service.
