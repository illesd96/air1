# Hooks for CANT 10

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

An Italian flying boat where four passengers floated inside and the pilot sat on top in the open.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1920s Trieste, CANT built the CANT 10 — a four-passenger flying boat biplane with a pusher engine slung between the wings and an open pilot cockpit perched above the hull. Passengers enjoyed an enclosed cabin while the pilot took the spray. It was charming, impractical, and quintessentially Italian Adriatic aviation.
