# Hooks for Comac C909

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

China's first homegrown regional jet — and it took 22 years to actually sell one outside China.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When the ARJ21 first flew in 2008, it was supposed to be China's commercial aviation coming-of-age. Renamed the C909, it's a 78–90-seat regional jet that's been quietly building hours on Chinese routes for years. Western certification still hasn't come. But more than 160 are now flying, and Indonesia's TransNusa became the first foreign customer.
