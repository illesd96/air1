The Comac C909 — known until 2024 as the ARJ21 Xiangfeng — is China's first commercial jet airliner. A 78–90 seat regional jet that first flew in 2008, it took years to certify, longer to export, and is now finally building a fleet inside and outside China. Indonesia's TransNusa was the first foreign customer.
