# Hooks for Concorde

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

London to New York in three hours, at twice the speed of sound — and we voluntarily gave it up in 2003.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

On October 24, 2003, a delta-winged needle painted in British Airways colors touched down at Heathrow and supersonic passenger travel died with it. The crash at Gonesse, the engine ban over land, the fuel crisis, the seats more expensive than a small car — Concorde's death wasn't one cause. It was a perfect storm two decades in the making.
