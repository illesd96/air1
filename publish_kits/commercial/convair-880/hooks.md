# Hooks for Convair 880

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

This jet was the fastest airliner in the world — and it bankrupted Convair.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1960, the Convair 880 cruised at 615 mph — faster than any commercial jet flying. It was sleek, narrow, and beautifully aggressive. It was also a financial catastrophe so severe it ended Convair's commercial airliner business forever. Only 65 were built, and General Dynamics absorbed losses estimated at over $400 million in 1960s money.
