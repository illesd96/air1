# Hooks for Convair CV-440 Metropolitan

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The DC-3 replacement nobody remembers — but Lufthansa flew it for over a decade.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1955, Convair launched the CV-440 Metropolitan as the final refinement of their pressurized twin piston airliner family. It was meant to replace the DC-3 on short-haul routes worldwide. Lufthansa, SAS, and Swissair all bought it. Then the jet age arrived, and within a decade the Metropolitan was relegated to cargo runs and African bush ops.
