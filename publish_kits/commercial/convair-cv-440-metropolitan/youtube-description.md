The Convair CV-440 Metropolitan is the final and most refined of Convair's pressurized piston twin airliner family. Launched in 1955 to replace the DC-3, it found buyers in Lufthansa, SAS, and Swissair before jets made it instantly obsolete. Many soldiered on for decades as freighters and bush planes.
