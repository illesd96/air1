# Hooks for Embraer E-Jet

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Brazil's aircraft maker was almost shut down by the government — and then built the regional jet that beat Bombardier.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1994 Embraer was a near-bankrupt state-owned mess that the Brazilian government privatized in desperation. A decade later, the E-Jet family — E170, E175, E190, E195 — was outselling the Bombardier CRJ and rewriting what regional airliners could do. Four-abreast seats, no middle, full stand-up cabins. Brazil took on Canada in the regional skies. And won.
