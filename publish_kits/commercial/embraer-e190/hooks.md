# Hooks for Embraer E190

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

JetBlue ordered 100 of these from a Brazilian factory and proved that a 100-seat regional jet could fly mainline routes profitably.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Most regional jets are scaled-down compromises. The Embraer E190 was different — it was a clean-sheet 100-seat twinjet designed for proper mainline thin routes, with a real cabin, four-abreast seats, and no middle seat anywhere on board. JetBlue saw it, ordered 100, and launched the modern hub-bypass airline business. Breeze copied the playbook two decades later. The E190 is the quiet revolution in your last flight from Boston.
