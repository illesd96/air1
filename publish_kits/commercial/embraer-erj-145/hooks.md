# Hooks for Embraer ERJ 145

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

This Brazilian jet quietly turned Embraer into the world's third-biggest planemaker.

The ERJ 145 not only saved Embraer — it humiliated every American regional carrier into ditching turboprops and quietly built the largest commuter jet fleet in aviation history.
