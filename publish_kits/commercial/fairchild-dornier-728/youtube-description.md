The Fairchild Dornier 728 was a 70-seat regional jet developed in Germany with American investment. 125 firm orders. A flown prototype. Lufthansa CityLine and Crossair lined up to take delivery. Then the company collapsed in 2002, and not a single aircraft was ever delivered. We walk through the program, the orders, and the bankruptcy that buried it.
