# Hooks for Farman F.70

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A 1920 French biplane airliner with the pilot exposed to the wind while passengers sat inside.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1920, Farman built the F.70 — a small biplane mail and passenger plane that put paying customers inside an enclosed cabin while leaving the pilot stuck in an open cockpit ahead of the wing. It was the smaller sibling to the legendary F.60 Goliath, and it quietly hauled French mail and passengers through the dawn of European commercial aviation.
