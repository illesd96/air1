# Hooks for Fokker 100

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Fokker's last airliner was a sales hit. Then in 1996 the Dutch government refused to bail them out and the entire Dutch civil aerospace industry died overnight.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Fokker 100 should have been a triumph: a clean-sheet 100-seat twinjet, hundreds sold to American Airlines, KLM, US Air. It was outselling its competitors. Then a strong guilder, a thin profit margin, and a Dutch coalition government that wouldn't pick up the phone in March 1996 sent Fokker into bankruptcy. The Fokker 100 fleet flew on. The Dutch airliner industry never did.
