# Hooks for Fokker 50

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Dutch engineering took an old design, polished it, and quietly built the best F27 ever made.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

By 1985, Fokker's legendary F27 Friendship was 30 years old. Instead of starting over, Fokker reskinned it with PW125 engines, six-blade props, and a glass cockpit — and called it the Fokker 50. It was the F27 perfected, but airlines wanted jets. Fokker collapsed in 1996, but the 50 still flies for Ethiopian and a handful of bush operators.
