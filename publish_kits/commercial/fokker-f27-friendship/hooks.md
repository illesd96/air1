# Hooks for Fokker F27 Friendship

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Netherlands built the best-selling postwar turboprop airliner in the world — and you have probably never heard its name.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

1958. Fokker — a Dutch company that hadn't built a real airliner since before the war — launched the F27 Friendship. Twin Rolls-Royce Darts, high wing, pressurized cabin, big oval windows. It became the most numerous postwar aircraft built in the Netherlands and the best-selling European turboprop airliner in history. Even Fairchild built it under license in America. And then jets came, and Fokker quietly slid toward bankruptcy.
