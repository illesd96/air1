# Hooks for Handley Page Dart Herald

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The British DC-3 replacement that arrived a decade too late — and bankrupted Handley Page.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1955, Handley Page launched the HPR.7 Dart Herald — a turboprop airliner aimed squarely at replacing the Douglas DC-3. But it took years to get to market, and by the time it arrived, Fokker's F27 and Avro's 748 had already won every contract. Only a handful sold. The disappointment contributed directly to Handley Page's collapse in 1970.
