# Hooks for Handley Page Hermes

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Britain's post-war flagship airliner had a problem: the prototype crashed on its first flight.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

December 1945. Britain needs to compete with American airliners flooding the post-war market. Handley Page rolls out the Hermes — a four-engine piston airliner meant to fly BOAC across the Empire. The prototype lifted off, pitched up, stalled, and crashed within minutes. The program survived, but barely. By 1964 every Hermes was retired. This is the story of a British airliner that was always one step behind the Americans.
