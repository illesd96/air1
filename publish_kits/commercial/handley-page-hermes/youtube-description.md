The Handley Page HP.81 Hermes was Britain's attempt to compete with the Douglas DC-6 and Lockheed Constellation. Its prototype crashed on first flight. The redesign took years. By the time it entered service with BOAC, the jet age was around the corner. We walk through the crash, the redesign, and the slow fade to retirement in 1964.
