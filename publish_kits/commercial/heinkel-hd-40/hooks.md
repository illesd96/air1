# Hooks for Heinkel HD 40

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Heinkel built a biplane in 1927 with one job: deliver a Berlin newspaper.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1927, the Berlin tabloid B.Z. wanted same-day distribution across Germany. They commissioned Heinkel to build them an airplane. The HD 40 was the result — a single-bay biplane built around one purpose: carry stacks of newspapers. It was Heinkel's quiet bet on a commercial niche that newspapers themselves would soon abandon. The HD 40 vanished from history. So did the idea of newspapers chartering their own air fleets.
