# Hooks for Ilyushin Il-76

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

It's a Soviet jet that takes off from dirt strips with 50 tonnes of cargo — and it's still doing it in 2026.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When the Soviets needed to deliver heavy machinery to Siberia, the Arctic, and Africa, they built the Il-76: four turbofans, a glazed nose for the navigator, multi-bogey landing gear that lets it operate from unpaved strips, and 50 tonnes of payload. Half a century later, it's the United Nations' first call for disaster relief and remains in active military service across 38 countries.
