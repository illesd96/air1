# Hooks for Irkut MC-21

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Russia's first 21st-century airliner — and sanctions forced them to rebuild it from scratch.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Irkut MC-21 was Russia's bold attempt to challenge the 737 and A320. It first flew in 2017 with American-made carbon composite wings and Pratt & Whitney engines. Then sanctions hit. Russia had to reinvent both the composite wing manufacturing process and the engine — switching to the all-Russian PD-14. The project now exists in a parallel aviation universe.
