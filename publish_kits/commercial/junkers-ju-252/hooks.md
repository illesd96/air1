# Hooks for Junkers Ju 252

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Nazi cargo plane meant to replace the Ju 52 — and Germany only built fifteen.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1942, Junkers flew the Ju 252 — a sleek three-engine transport intended to finally replace the corrugated old Ju 52/3m in commercial airline service. It had retractable gear, pressurized cabin features, and modern lines. But the war crushed Junkers's commercial ambitions, and only fifteen were ever built — all for Luftwaffe cargo duty.
