The Junkers Ju 252 was Germany's elegant attempt to finally replace the legendary Ju 52/3m. First flown in 1941, it was a modern three-engine transport with retractable gear and refined aerodynamics. But the war prevented mass production, and only fifteen Ju 252s ever flew — all in Luftwaffe cargo service.
