The Kawasaki KH-4 is the Japanese refinement of the American Bell 47 — and arguably the better aircraft. Kawasaki had been licensing Bell 47s since 1952, but in the 1960s they redesigned the cabin entirely, replacing the iconic bubble with a fully enclosed four-seat fuselage and a bench seat behind the pilot.
