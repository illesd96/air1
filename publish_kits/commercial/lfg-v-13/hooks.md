# Hooks for LFG V 13

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A 1921 German seaplane airliner with three biplane wings — and almost nobody bought it.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The LFG V 13 Strela was a tiny German three-bay biplane seaplane airliner produced in the early 1920s for a market that barely existed. Four passengers sat in an enclosed cabin between twin pontoons; the pilot sat outside. LFG built only a handful before realizing nobody wanted to commute across Berlin's lakes by air.
