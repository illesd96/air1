The LFG V 13 Strela was a small German biplane seaplane airliner produced by Luft-Fahrzeug-Gesellschaft in the early 1920s. A three-bay biplane with twin pontoons and an enclosed four-passenger cabin, it tried to serve a Weimar-era German lake aviation market that never materialized. Only a small handful were ever built.
