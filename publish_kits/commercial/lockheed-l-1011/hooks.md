# Hooks for Lockheed L-1011 TriStar

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Technically better than the DC-10 in every way — and it bankrupted Lockheed out of commercial aviation forever.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The L-1011 TriStar had autoland in 1972, an S-duct that quieted its center engine, and Rolls-Royce RB.211 high-bypass turbofans nobody else had. Then Rolls-Royce went bankrupt mid-development, the British government had to nationalize them, deliveries slipped, the DC-10 stole the orders — and Lockheed lost $2.5 billion. They never built a commercial airliner again.
