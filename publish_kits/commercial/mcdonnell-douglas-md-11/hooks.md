# Hooks for McDonnell Douglas MD-11

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

It promised the range of a 747 with three engines — and missed the spec by 13%.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When McDonnell Douglas launched the MD-11 in 1986, they promised airlines a stretched, more efficient DC-10 that could cross the Pacific. Singapore Airlines ordered 20. Then the deliveries came in 13% short of the promised range. Singapore cancelled. So did others. McDonnell Douglas paid millions in compensation, the program never recovered, and the MD-11 lived out its life as a FedEx and Lufthansa Cargo freighter.
