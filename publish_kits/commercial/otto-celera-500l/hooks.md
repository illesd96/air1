# Hooks for Otto Celera 500L

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

An American startup says they built a bullet-shaped propeller plane that gets 8x better fuel economy than a regional jet.

2019. Otto Aviation flies a strange torpedo-shaped aircraft over Southern California. It's the Celera 500L — a six-seat business plane with a single diesel piston engine in pusher configuration. Otto claims laminar-flow fuselage shaping cuts drag so dramatically that the Celera burns one-eighth the fuel of a comparable jet. By 2026 it's still pre-production. The aviation press is divided: revolutionary breakthrough, or carefully managed hype? We dig into what's verified.
