# Hooks for Saab 2000

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Saab built a turboprop faster than some regional jets — and nobody bought it.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1992, Saab unveiled the 2000: a stretched, ultra-fast turboprop that could cruise at 685 km/h, just shy of regional jet speeds. It was beautifully engineered. It was also expensive, complicated, and arrived just as airlines were switching to actual jets. Only 63 were ever built. Saab quit civil aviation in 1999.
