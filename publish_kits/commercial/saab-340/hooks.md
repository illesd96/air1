# Hooks for Saab 340

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A Swedish-American turboprop that's still hauling passengers 40 years after its first flight.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Born from an unlikely partnership between Saab and Fairchild in the 1980s, the Saab 340 was supposed to be a quick regional commuter. Four decades later, 240 of them are still flying for 34 different operators worldwide — outlasting their Fairchild partner, outlasting Saab's entire civil aircraft division, and outlasting most of their competitors.
