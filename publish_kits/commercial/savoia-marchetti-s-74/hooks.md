# Hooks for Savoia-Marchetti S.74

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Mussolini's state airline had its own private airliner — and most people have never seen a photo of it.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Italy in 1934 was building an empire — and Mussolini's state airline, Ala Littoria, needed an aircraft to match. Enter the Savoia-Marchetti S.74: a four-engine high-wing airliner with a panoramic cabin built for the prestige routes from Rome to North Africa. It was Italian engineering at its most theatrical, and it disappeared from aviation history the moment WWII rolled over it.
