# Hooks for Savoia-Marchetti SM.73

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Mussolini's Italy built this three-engined airliner — and Belgium bought it before WWII.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1934, Savoia-Marchetti produced the S.73, an unusual three-engine, fixed-gear airliner that became the backbone of Ala Littoria, Italy's national carrier. Only 48 were built, but seven were licensed to Belgium's SABCA for SABENA — making it one of the rare Italian designs exported into Northern Europe before the Second World War redrew aviation.
