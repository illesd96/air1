The Savoia-Marchetti S.73 was an unusual three-engined, fixed-gear airliner built for Italy's Ala Littoria in 1934. Only 48 were built, but it became one of the rare Italian designs exported pre-WWII — with seven units licensed-built by Belgium's SABCA for SABENA service.
