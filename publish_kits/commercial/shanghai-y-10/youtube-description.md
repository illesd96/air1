The Shanghai Y-10 — or Yun-10 — was China's first attempt at a domestic jet airliner. It used the Boeing 707-320C as its design reference and first flew in 1980. The political winds changed, funding evaporated, and the program died. We walk through the engineering, the politics, and how the Y-10 quietly set the stage for today's C919.
