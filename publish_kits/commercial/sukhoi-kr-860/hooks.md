# Hooks for Sukhoi KR-860

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Russia tried to build an 860-passenger double-decker jumbo — and only a model showed up at the Paris Air Show.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

1999 Paris Air Show. Sukhoi unveils a 1/24 scale model of the KR-860 'Wings of Russia' — a proposed wide-body, double-deck superjumbo with seating for 860 passengers. It's a direct competitor pitch against what would become the A380. The full-scale aircraft never gets built. Russia's commercial aviation funding never materializes. The model gathers dust. And the dream of a Russian double-decker dies before metal is ever cut.
