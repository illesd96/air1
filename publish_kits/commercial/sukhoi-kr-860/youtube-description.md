The Sukhoi KR-860 'Wings of Russia' — originally the SKD-717 — was Russia's proposed answer to the A380. A double-deck, wide-body superjumbo with seating for up to 860 passengers. A 1/24 scale model was shown at Paris in 1999. No full aircraft was ever built. We trace the design, the politics, and why post-Soviet Russia couldn't fund a jumbo.
