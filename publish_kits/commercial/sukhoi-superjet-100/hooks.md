# Hooks for Sukhoi Superjet 100

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Russia's first post-Soviet airliner had French engines, Italian avionics, and Boeing customer support — and sanctions in 2022 grounded it everywhere outside Russia.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Sukhoi Superjet 100 was supposed to prove Russia could play in the global regional jet market. Built in partnership with Boeing, powered by PowerJet engines from a French-Russian joint venture, certified by EASA — it almost worked. Then Russia invaded Ukraine, sanctions cut off every Western part on the airplane, and operators outside Russia couldn't get spares. The SSJ100's international career ended in months.
