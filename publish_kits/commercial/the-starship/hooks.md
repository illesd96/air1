# Hooks for The Starship

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

In 1975, rock stars chartered a private Boeing 720 with a fireplace, an organ, and a waterbed.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

1975. Bobby Sherman and his manager buy a used United Airlines Boeing 720, rip out the seats, and turn it into the most outrageous touring vehicle in rock history. Led Zeppelin flew it. The Rolling Stones flew it. Elton John, Deep Purple, Alice Cooper. It had a fireplace. An electric organ. A waterbed. Painted across the side: THE STARSHIP. For five years it was the most extravagant private jet on Earth — and most people today don't even know it existed.
