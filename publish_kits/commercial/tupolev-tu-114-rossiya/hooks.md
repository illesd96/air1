# Hooks for Tupolev Tu-114 Rossiya

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Soviet bomber wings, propeller-driven, that flew Khrushchev to America at 540 mph.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1957, the Tupolev Tu-114 took flight — a stretched, civilianized version of the Tu-95 strategic bomber. It used the same swept wings, the same Kuznetsov NK-12 turboprops, and the same contra-rotating props. The result was the fastest propeller-driven airliner ever built — a title it still holds — and the plane Khrushchev rode to JFK's America in 1959.
