The Tupolev Tu-114 Rossiya is a near-mythical Soviet airliner — a stretched, civilianized version of the Tu-95 strategic bomber. With contra-rotating Kuznetsov NK-12 turboprops, it remains the fastest propeller-driven aircraft ever built and held the longest-range airliner title in 1960. It famously carried Khrushchev to the US in 1959.
