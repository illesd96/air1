# Hooks for Tupolev Tu-144

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Soviets beat Concorde to supersonic flight by two months — then their prototype broke apart over the Paris Air Show.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

On December 31, 1968, a Tu-144 broke the sound barrier two months before Concorde. Western press called it Concordski and accused the Soviets of espionage. Then at the 1973 Paris Air Show, in front of 350,000 spectators, a Tu-144 broke up in midair. By 1983 it was retired from passenger service after just 55 flights. This is the cautionary tale of being first.
