# Hooks for Tupolev Tu-154

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

If you flew anywhere in the Soviet Union between 1972 and the year 2000, there's a one-in-three chance you flew on this exact plane.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Three Kuznetsov turbofans hanging off the tail. A swept wing built to handle ice, slush, and runways that were basically frozen mud. The Tupolev Tu-154 was the airliner of the entire Eastern Bloc — Aeroflot, Interflug, Balkan, Cubana, Tarom — and it flew half of every passenger Aeroflot ever carried. Then capitalism arrived, fuel got expensive, and the Tu-154 quietly died.
