# Hooks for Tupolev Tu-244

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Russia's second supersonic airliner was supposed to burn cryogenic fuel — then 1993 happened and the entire program died.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Tu-144 was a supersonic disaster — too noisy, too fuel-hungry, retired by 1978. So Tupolev tried again. The Tu-244 was supposed to be everything the Tu-144 wasn't: 300 passengers, 10,000 kilometers of range, cryogenic liquid-hydrogen or liquid-methane fuel, environmental compatibility. Then the Soviet Union collapsed. Funding dried up. The Tu-244 was cancelled in 1993 with not a single full prototype built. It's the supersonic future Russia almost had — and lost overnight.
