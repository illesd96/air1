The Tupolev Tu-244 was a proposed supersonic transport developed from the Tu-144. It would have carried 300 passengers up to 10,000 kilometers using cryogenic fuel — likely liquid hydrogen or methane. The Soviet Union collapsed. The program was cancelled in 1993. We walk through what made the Tu-244 radical, and why it never had a chance.
