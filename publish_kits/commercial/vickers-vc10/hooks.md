# Hooks for Vickers VC10

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Britain built an airliner specifically to fly out of Nairobi in summer heat with a full load — and the RAF kept flying it as a tanker until 2013.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Hot-and-high. Two words that destroyed early jet airliners trying to operate out of Johannesburg, Nairobi, and the BOAC Empire routes through Africa and the Middle East. So Vickers designed an airliner around those airports: four engines clustered on the tail, a clean wing, and brutal takeoff thrust. The VC10 was the best airliner BOAC ever had — and it killed Vickers because nobody else needed it.
