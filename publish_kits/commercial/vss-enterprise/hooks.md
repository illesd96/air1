# Hooks for VSS Enterprise

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Virgin Galactic's first spaceplane broke apart in mid-air in 2014 — and the cause was a single misplaced lever.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

October 31, 2014. VSS Enterprise — Virgin Galactic's first SpaceShipTwo — is on its fourth powered test flight. The co-pilot unlocks the feathering re-entry system thirteen seconds too early. Aerodynamic forces tear the aircraft apart over the Mojave Desert. One pilot dies. The other survives by being ejected. The program survives, but it's set back by years. The Enterprise was supposed to be the first of five commercial spacecraft. Only one other was ever built.
