VSS Enterprise was the first SpaceShipTwo built by Scaled Composites for Virgin Galactic. On October 31, 2014, the co-pilot unlocked the feathering system early during powered ascent and the aircraft broke up over the Mojave Desert. We walk through the design, the accident, and what it meant for the future of commercial sub-orbital spaceflight.
