# Hooks for Yakovlev SJ-100

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Russia's first all-new airliner in 30 years — and Western sanctions nearly killed it twice.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Sukhoi Superjet was Russia's bold attempt to re-enter the global airliner market after the Soviet collapse. Built with French engines, Italian avionics, and American flight controls, it first flew in 2008. Then came sanctions, the 2012 Mount Salak crash, and Western suppliers walking out. Rebranded as the Yakovlev SJ-100, it's now in a forced re-engineering with Russian-only parts.
