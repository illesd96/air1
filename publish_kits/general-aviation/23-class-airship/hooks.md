# Hooks for 23 class airship

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Vickers rigid airships Britain built to fight U-boats in WWI.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Designed by H.B. Pratt and a young Barnes Wallis at Vickers in 1916, the 23-class were Britain's improved answer to the troubled 9r. Four were built. They patrolled the North Sea looking for U-boats, taught the RNAS how to fly rigid airships, and seeded the engineers who would later build the R100.
