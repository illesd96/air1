# Hooks for Aerion SBJ

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The billion-dollar supersonic bizjet that never built a single airframe.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Aerion promised the first private supersonic jet, Mach 1.6, a NASA-developed laminar flow wing and Atlantic crossings in half the time. Billionaire Robert Bass funded it. Lockheed and Boeing partnered with it. Two decades and over a billion dollars later, in May 2021, Aerion shut the doors with nothing flying.
