# Hooks for Aeroprakt A-32 Vixxen

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A Ukrainian two-seater that lands shorter than most ultralights and cruises like an LSA.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Designed by Yuri Yakovlev in Kyiv, the Aeroprakt A-32 is a high-wing tricycle-gear two-seater built from welded steel tube and aluminum skin. In Australia it is sold as the Vixxen. STOL manners, big cabin, Rotax 912 power. Quietly outstanding.
