# Hooks for AMD Alarus

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Canadian-designed trainer Mexico's air force built by the dozen.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Aircraft Manufacturing and Design started in Quebec in the late 1990s with a clean-sheet, all-metal trainer called the CH 2000 Alarus. After AMD moved south to Eastman, Georgia, the Mexican Air Force took 25 of them for primary flight training. Quiet success, almost no fame.
