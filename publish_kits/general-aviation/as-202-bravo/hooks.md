# Hooks for AS 202 Bravo

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Italian-Swiss aerobatic trainer flown by half a dozen air forces.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Flug- und Fahrzeugwerke Altenrhein partnered with Savoia-Marchetti in 1969 on a two-seat, low-wing trainer for European clubs and air forces. Iraqi, Indonesian, Ugandan and several other air forces ordered it. Switzerland never adopted it. The AS-202 Bravo is the quiet alpine cousin nobody quite remembers.
