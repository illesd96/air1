# Hooks for Auster Autocar

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Britain's postwar answer to the Piper Cub, built in Leicestershire.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

From the Rearsby factory in Leicestershire, Auster Aircraft built the four-seat J/5 Autocar from 1949: a high-wing taildragger descended from the prewar British Taylorcraft licence. Only about 28 left the line. Today the Autocar is one of the rarer British four-seaters and a cult favourite at vintage fly-ins.
