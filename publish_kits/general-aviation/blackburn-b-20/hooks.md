# Hooks for Blackburn B-20

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The flying boat with a hull that pulled itself up out of the water.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Blackburn's John Douglas Rennie patented a retractable pontoon hull that doubled as the planing surface, then built it for real in 1940. The B-20's lower hull retracted upward in flight to clean up the airframe. One prototype, lost in a flight test crash, ended a quietly clever wartime experiment.
