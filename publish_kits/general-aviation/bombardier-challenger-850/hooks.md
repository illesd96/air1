# Hooks for Bombardier Challenger 850

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

It's a 50-seat regional jet. With twelve seats.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Bombardier looked at the CRJ200 regional airliner on the assembly line in Montreal and asked a strange question: what if we sold the same airframe to one customer, with a sofa instead of fifty seats? The result was the Challenger 850 — a super-midsize business jet with an honest-to-goodness airline cabin, transcontinental range, and the kind of presence on a corporate ramp that smaller bizjets simply can't match.
