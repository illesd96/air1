# Hooks for Bugatti Model 100

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Ettore Bugatti built an air racer in 1939. The Germans invaded before he could fly it.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Designed by Louis de Monge and Ettore Bugatti for the 1939 Deutsch de la Meurthe Cup, the Bugatti Model 100 used twin centerline-thrust engines, a forward-swept wing, and automotive-grade craftsmanship. It never flew. A modern replica did, and it lived up to the legend.
