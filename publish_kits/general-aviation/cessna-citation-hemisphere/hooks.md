# Hooks for Cessna Citation Hemisphere

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Cessna jet that died because its French engine wouldn't show up.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Announced in 2015, Cessna's Hemisphere was supposed to be the biggest Citation ever: Mach 0.90, 4,500 nautical miles, transcontinental at flagship cabin width. Then Safran's Silvercrest engine missed every milestone. By 2018 Textron quietly paused the program. The mockup is all that flew.
