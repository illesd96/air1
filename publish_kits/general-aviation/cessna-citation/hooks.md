# Hooks for Cessna Citation

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Cessna made the business jet you could actually afford. Then they made 8,000 of them.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1972, Cessna did to business aviation what they had already done to general aviation: they made it accessible. The Citation family is now the largest business jet fleet on Earth, with over 8,000 delivered across thirty-plus variants. In this video, how a slow, simple little jet quietly took over the segment — and why the Citation name still sells the airplane.
