# Hooks for Cessna CitationJet

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The smallest, cheapest, easiest Citation you can own. And they sold thousands.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

By the late 1980s, Cessna realized the cheapest way to get more people into a Citation was to build a smaller, simpler one. The CitationJet shipped in 1993 with two Williams FJ44s, a straight-tail T-tail design, and pricing that owner-operators could actually justify. In this video, the entry point to the entire Citation family — and the airplane that made the very light jet category make sense.
