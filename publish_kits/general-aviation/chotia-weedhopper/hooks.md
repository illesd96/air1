# Hooks for Chotia Weedhopper

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The two-axis-control ultralight you could build in a weekend in 1977.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

John Chotia drew the Weedhopper at the height of the 1970s ultralight boom: high wing, tractor configuration, tricycle gear, and only two-axis controls. The kit assembled in 25 to 30 hours and sold by the thousands. Decades later, restored Weedhoppers still turn up at every ultralight gathering in America.
