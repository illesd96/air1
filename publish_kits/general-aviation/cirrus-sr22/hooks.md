# Hooks for Cirrus SR22

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 2002, Cirrus did something the FAA had never certified before: they bolted a rocket-deployed parachute to a four-seat composite single. Two decades later, that handle has saved over 240 lives and rewritten what general aviation considers acceptable risk. Here's why the SR22 is the best-selling piston single of the 21st century, and the arguments pilots still have about CAPS at 800 feet.
