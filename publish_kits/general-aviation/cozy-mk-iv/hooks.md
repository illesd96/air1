# Hooks for Cozy MK IV

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A four-seat canard you build from plans, not a kit.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Nat Puffer started with Burt Rutan's Long-EZ and stretched it into a four-seat canard you build from raw materials on a garage floor. Aircraft Spruce sells the plans. Twenty-plus years of builders have flown it across continents on Lycoming power.
