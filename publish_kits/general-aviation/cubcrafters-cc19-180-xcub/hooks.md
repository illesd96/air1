# Hooks for CubCrafters CC19-180 XCub

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The 21st-century Super Cub, hand-built in Yakima.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

CubCrafters spent decades restoring real Super Cubs before drawing the CC19 XCub from scratch in 2016. Same Cub silhouette, all-new chromoly fuselage, 180-horsepower Lycoming, glass panel, three-blade prop. It is the modern bushplane reinterpretation that won FAA Part 23 certification and made backcountry pilots reach for their cheque books.
