# Hooks for Dassault Falcon 8X

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Falcon 8X is what happens when Dassault takes the 7X — already an extraordinary airplane — and stretches it for another 500 nautical miles of range and a cabin that goes after the very top of the segment. Three Pratt & Whitney engines, a fly-by-wire wing, and the kind of quietness in cruise that comes from being engineered by people who like quiet airplanes. In this video, the modern Dassault flagship trijet.
