The DF-17 is a Chinese medium-range ballistic missile system designed to carry the DF-ZF hypersonic glide vehicle. The booster lobs the HGV into the upper atmosphere; the glider then skips and maneuvers at hypersonic speeds — reported between Mach 5 and Mach 10 — until impact. The weapon system was likely operational by October 2019.
