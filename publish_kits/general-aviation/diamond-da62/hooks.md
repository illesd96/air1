# Hooks for Diamond DA62

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Two diesel engines. Seven seats. Sips Jet-A.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Diamond Aircraft started with sailplanes in Austria. Then they built composite singles. Then, in 2015, they did something nobody else was doing seriously: a clean-sheet, twin-diesel, seven-seat piston twin that burns Jet-A and gets King Air-class cabin room out of two Austro engines. The DA62 isn't the fastest twin. It's something more interesting — a glimpse of what GA looks like after avgas.
