The Erla 5 was a small, single-seat sport aircraft built in Germany in the early 1930s. A trainer variant, the Me 5a, was demonstrated on long-distance flights as late as 1939 but failed to attract orders before history overran it. In this video, a tiny pre-war German light sport airplane that briefly tried to find a market.
