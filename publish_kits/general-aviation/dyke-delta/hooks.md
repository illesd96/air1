# Hooks for Dyke Delta

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The four-seat delta-wing homebuilt with folding wings that hinge over the fuselage.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

John Dyke designed his JD-2 Delta in 1960s Ohio: a four-seat tailless delta with retractable gear, steel tube spaceframe and fabric-and-fiberglass skin. The wings fold flat over the fuselage for trailering. Half a century later, the Dyke Delta is still one of homebuilding's most original airframes.
