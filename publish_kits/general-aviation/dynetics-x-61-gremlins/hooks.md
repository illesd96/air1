# Hooks for Dynetics X-61 Gremlins

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

DARPA wanted recoverable drone swarms. This is what they got.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 2016, DARPA put out a brief: build us small, cheap drones that can be launched from a transport aircraft, fly a mission, and then get scooped back up in midair by the same airplane. The Dynetics X-61 Gremlins took on the impossible part — the midair retrieval. In November 2021, a C-130 actually caught a returning Gremlin in flight. The future of attritable, reusable UAVs started there.
