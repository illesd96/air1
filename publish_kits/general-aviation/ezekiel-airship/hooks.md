# Hooks for Ezekiel Airship

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The 1902 Texas airship inspired by the Book of Ezekiel.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Baptist minister Burrell Cannon built an airship in Pittsburg, Texas in 1902 with four paddle wheels arranged as wheels-within-wheels, just like the visions in the Book of Ezekiel. Unverified local legend says it flew briefly before the Wright Brothers. Confirmed history says it ended up in a ditch and the rest is folklore.
