# Hooks for Facebook Aquila

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Facebook built a solar-powered drone the size of a 737 to beam internet. Then quietly killed it.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Aquila was Meta's bet on connecting the unconnected: a 42-metre solar-electric flying wing meant to loiter for months as an atmospheric satellite. It flew in 2016, broke its landing gear, flew again in 2017, then died in a board meeting in 2018.
