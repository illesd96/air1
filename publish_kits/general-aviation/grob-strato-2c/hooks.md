# Hooks for Grob Strato 2C

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The German altitude-record breaker that set a record on its final flight.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Grob Aircraft built the Strato 2C in the 1990s on a German Aerospace Center contract to fly atmospheric research at 24 kilometres. Two turbocharged piston engines, composite wings as long as a Boeing 737, and a single airframe. On its last flight it set the absolute piston altitude record. Then the programme died.
