# Hooks for Hafner Rotabuggy

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A Willys Jeep with a rotor on top, dropped from a Whitley bomber.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Raoul Hafner glued a rotor mast to a standard Willys MB Jeep in 1943 so the British could air-drop off-road vehicles into combat as autogyros. The Rotabuggy actually flew. It towed behind a Whitley to 70 miles per hour and landed. Then the C-47 Skytrain arrived with hold space and the project quietly stopped.
