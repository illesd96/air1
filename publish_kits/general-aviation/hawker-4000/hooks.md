# Hooks for Hawker 4000

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A super-midsize composite bizjet that should have ruled the segment. What happened?

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When Raytheon launched the Hawker Horizon program in the late 1990s, the plan was simple: build the first all-composite super-midsize business jet and take the segment from the Falcon 50 and Citation X. The program slipped, costs ballooned, certification dragged, and by the time the Hawker 4000 entered service, the world had changed. In this video, the story of one of the most ambitious bizjet programs that never quite landed.
