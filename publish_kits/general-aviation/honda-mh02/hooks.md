# Hooks for Honda MH02

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The composite Honda bizjet that taught Honda how to build the HondaJet.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Honda flew the experimental MH02 in 1993 in cooperation with Mississippi State University. It was the world's first all-composite business jet and the rolling test bed for the over-the-wing engine mount, fuselage layup and tail design that would resurface twenty years later in the production HondaJet.
