# Hooks for Light Helicopter Experimental

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The 1980s US Army helicopter program that birthed the Comanche.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Begun in 1982, the Light Helicopter Experimental program set out to replace the AH-1 Cobra and OH-58 Kiowa with a single stealthy reconnaissance helicopter. Boeing-Sikorsky won with the RAH-66 Comanche. The Army cancelled the Comanche in 2004 after spending 7 billion dollars and a small fleet of demonstrators.
