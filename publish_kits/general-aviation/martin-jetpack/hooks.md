# Hooks for Martin Jetpack

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A New Zealand 'jetpack' that wasn't a jet, didn't sell, but actually flew.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Glenn Martin spent thirty years perfecting a ducted-fan personal aircraft he called a jetpack. The FAA classified it as an experimental ultralight airplane. It flew, it was sold to the Chinese, it ran out of runway as a product, but Martin proved a man can stand on two ducted fans and fly.
