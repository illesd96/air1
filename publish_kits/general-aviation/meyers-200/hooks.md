# Hooks for Meyers 200

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Built stronger than a Bonanza. Faster than a Mooney. And almost nobody remembers it.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In the late 1950s, a small Michigan manufacturer named Allen Meyers built a four-place retractable single that owners still describe as overbuilt in the best way. The Meyers 200 outclimbed and outran most of its competition, and the Aero Commander Lark that followed it kept the bones. In this video, one of the great forgotten high-performance singles of GA.
