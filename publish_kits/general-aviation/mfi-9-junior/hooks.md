# Hooks for MFI-9 Junior

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Swedish sportplane that ended up bombing a Nigerian oilfield.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Bjorn Andreasson drew the MFI-9 Junior in Sweden in 1958 as a clean-sheet two-seat light aircraft. Bolkow built it under licence in West Germany. Then Count von Rosen took five of them, armed them with rockets, and flew them as the Biafran Babies against Nigerian air bases in 1969. Unlikely doesn't cover it.
