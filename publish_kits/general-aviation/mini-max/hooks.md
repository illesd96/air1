# Hooks for Mini-MAX

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The 1984 plans-built ultralight you can fold up and trailer home.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Wayne Ison drew the Mini-MAX as a minimum-cost, minimum-skill, minimum-hangar wooden ultralight that an absolute beginner could finish in a garage. Forty years on, the TEAM Aircraft family still sells plans and kits, and the Mini-MAX remains one of the easiest legitimate homebuilts in the world.
