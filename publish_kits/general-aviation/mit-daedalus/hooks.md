# Hooks for MIT Daedalus

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

MIT students pedaled an airplane from Crete to Santorini in 1988.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

On 23 April 1988, Greek cycling champion Kanellos Kanellopoulos pedaled the MIT Daedalus 88 for three hours and 54 minutes, covering 115.11 kilometres from Heraklion toward Santorini before the tailboom snapped meters from the beach. The human-powered flight records still stand.
