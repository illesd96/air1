# Hooks for Moth

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The de Havilland that taught the entire 1920s to fly.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Geoffrey de Havilland's Moth series turned aviation from a military leftover into a hobby. The DH.60 Cipher, the Gipsy Moth, the Puss Moth and the immortal Tiger Moth defined British sport flying between the wars and still pack vintage fly-ins from Old Warden to Mascot today.
