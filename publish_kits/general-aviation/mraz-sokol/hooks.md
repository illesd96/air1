# Hooks for Mráz Sokol

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Designed in secret during Nazi occupation. Built the year after liberation.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In a country still under German occupation in 1944, an engineer named Zdeněk Rublič was quietly drawing a light aircraft he wasn't allowed to build. The moment Czechoslovakia was liberated, the Beneš-Mráz factory put the Sokol into production. In this video, a forgotten post-war European light aircraft that deserved a better market than it ever got.
