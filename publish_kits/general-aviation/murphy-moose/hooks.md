# Hooks for Murphy Moose

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A Canadian kitplane the size of a Beaver that you build in your garage.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Murphy Aircraft of Chilliwack, BC built the Moose for pilots who wanted bush-plane utility on a homebuilder budget. Six seats, big doors, M14P radial or Lycoming, and an undercarriage you can put on tundra tires, floats, or skis. The Canadian backcountry has been quietly testing them for thirty years.
