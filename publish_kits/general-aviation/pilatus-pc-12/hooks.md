# Hooks for Pilatus PC-12

The Pilatus PC-12 broke the rule that you needed two engines to carry serious people serious distances. Since 1994 the Swiss have been hand-building a pressurized turboprop that competes with twinjets on mission and beats them on operating cost. In this video, why the PC-12 became the operator's airplane — fractional, charter, owner-flown, and royal flight detachment.
