# Hooks for Piper PA-28 Cherokee

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Cessna had the 172. Piper said: low wing, one door, watch this.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1960, Piper needed an answer to the Cessna 172 and couldn't afford to lose. They built the PA-28 Cherokee — a low-wing, all-metal, tricycle gear trainer with a single door over the wing root. Sixty-five years and 32,000 airframes later, the Cherokee family is half of the world's flight schools. In this video, the trainer that refused to die.
