# Hooks for Piper J-3 Cub

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Yellow plane. Grass strip. The whole aviation thing started here.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Before the GI Bill, before the King Airs and the Cirrus parachutes, there was a little yellow taildragger that taught a country to fly. The Piper J-3 Cub trained tens of thousands of WWII pilots, populated the back-country, and made aviation feel possible for normal people. In this video, the most romanticized airplane in American GA — and the one most pilots still want to own.
