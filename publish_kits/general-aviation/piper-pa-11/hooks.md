# Hooks for Piper PA-11

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The post-war Cub Piper rebuilt to win back the family flyer market.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

By 1947 the J-3 Cub looked dated. Piper's answer was the PA-11 Cub Special: enclosed fuel in the wing, a 90-horsepower Continental up front, and a clean cowl. Soloed from the front seat. Fewer than 1,500 built, but the PA-11 paved the runway for the Super Cub that conquered the bush.
