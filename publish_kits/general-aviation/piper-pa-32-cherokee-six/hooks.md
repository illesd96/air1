# Hooks for Piper PA-32 Cherokee Six

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Six seats. One engine. One door. The pickup truck of GA.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Piper PA-32 Cherokee Six is the airplane you call when you need to move six adults, the dogs, the cooler, and a pair of skis — and you'd rather not bother with a twin. Piper stretched the Cherokee, dropped in a 260 or 300 horsepower engine, and accidentally built one of the most useful family airplanes in general aviation. In this video, the most pragmatic single Piper ever made.
