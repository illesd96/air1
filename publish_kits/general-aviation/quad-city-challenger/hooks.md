# Hooks for Quad City Challenger

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

An Illinois ultralight that quietly trained two generations of pilots.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Quad City Aircraft launched the Challenger in 1983 from Moline, Illinois. Pusher prop, tricycle gear, tandem seats, dacron-covered tube structure. Float-equipped, ski-equipped, single-seat ultralight or two-seat experimental: the Challenger has been the gateway drug for thousands of low-and-slow pilots.
