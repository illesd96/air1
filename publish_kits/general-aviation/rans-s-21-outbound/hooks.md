# Hooks for Rans S-21 Outbound

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Randy Schlitter's STOL answer to the modern Cub craze.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Introduced at Oshkosh in 2016, the Rans S-21 Outbound is a two-seat, side-by-side, all-metal STOL kit aimed squarely at the CubCrafters and Kitfox bushplane crowd. Quick-build kit, big tyres, slats, Vortex generators. Hays, Kansas keeps shipping them as fast as builders can pick them up.
