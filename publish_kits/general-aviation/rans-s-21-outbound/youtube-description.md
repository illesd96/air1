Rans Designs of Hays, Kansas introduced the S-21 Outbound at AirVenture in 2016 with one mission: take the Cub silhouette into the modern STOL kit market and beat CubCrafters on price. The airframe is side-by-side two-seat, high-wing, all-metal with a tube and fabric tail and is supplied as a quick-build kit that an experienced builder can finish in well under a thousand hours. The wing carries fixed leading-edge slats, big mechanical flaps, and supports tundra tyres on either trailing-link or sprung legs. Power options range from the carbureted 100-horsepower Rotax 912 up through the 180-horsepower Titan O-340 and Lycoming O-360. Randy Schlitter has stayed in Hays through nearly forty years of design work, and the Outbound has become his fastest-selling kit. We compare the Outbound to the CubCrafters EX-3 and the Kitfox STi, walk through the quick-build option sheet, look at performance numbers from real builders, and explain why so many homebuilders are picking the Outbound for their first bush kit.
