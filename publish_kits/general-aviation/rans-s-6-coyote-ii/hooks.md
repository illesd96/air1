# Hooks for Rans S-6 Coyote II

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Kansas-built two-seater that took the Cub formula and Rotax-ified it.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Randy Schlitter drew the S-6 in 1988 as a side-by-side, high-wing taildragger or tricycle for the European microlight class and the American kit market. Decades later, with thousands flying and Rotax 912 power available, the Coyote II is the workhorse of the Rans empire from Hays, Kansas.
