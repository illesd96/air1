# Hooks for Robinson R22

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1979, Frank Robinson started shipping a two-seat helicopter that almost nobody else in the industry believed in. Forty-five years later, the Robinson R22 is the most common rotorcraft trainer on Earth and the airframe that civilian helicopter aviation effectively built itself on. In this video, the tiny machine that made commercial rotor training possible — and the special FAA rules it earned.
