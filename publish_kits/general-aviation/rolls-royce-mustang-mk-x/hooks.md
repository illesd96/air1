# Hooks for Rolls-Royce Mustang Mk.X

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The British experiment that turned a good Mustang into the P-51 we remember.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Allison-engined Mustang I was fast at low altitude and ordinary above 15,000 feet. Rolls-Royce engineers thought the Merlin was the answer. They were right. Five Mustang Mk.X conversions in 1942 led directly to the Packard Merlin P-51B and the legend that followed.
