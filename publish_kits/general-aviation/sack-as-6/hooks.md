# Hooks for Sack AS-6

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Nazi-era flying saucer that hopped, wobbled, and never really flew.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Built privately on a German farm in 1944, the Sack AS-6 was an actual round-winged aircraft, hammered together from scavenged Messerschmitt Bf 109 parts. It taxied. It hopped. It crashed on its own undercarriage. And then it disappeared into legend.
