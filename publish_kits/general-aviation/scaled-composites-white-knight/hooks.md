## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 2003, Burt Rutan rolled out a jet-powered carrier aircraft that looked like a flying biplane truss. The White Knight existed for exactly one purpose: to carry SpaceShipOne to 50,000 feet, drop it, and watch a private spacecraft win the Ansari X PRIZE. In October 2004 it did. The first privately funded crewed spaceflight in history. White Knight is the unsung hero of the moment commercial spaceflight became real.
