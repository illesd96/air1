The Sikorsky S-69, also called the XH-59A, first flew in 1973 with U.S. Army and NASA funding behind it. Two rigid coaxial rotors, no tail rotor, two J60 turbojets bolted on later for pure forward thrust — and a top speed of 238 knots that no production helicopter has matched since.
