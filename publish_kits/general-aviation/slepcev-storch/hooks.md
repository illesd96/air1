# Hooks for Slepcev Storch

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A three-quarter scale Fieseler Storch you can build at home.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Nestor Slepcev built his first Storch replica in Australia in the 1990s, scaled to 3/4 of the wartime original. Today Storch Aircraft Serbia produces type-certified, kit, and ultralight versions, and the slow-flying Slepcev shows up at every STOL event with the kind of presence the original would have recognized.
