# Hooks for SNECMA Coléoptère

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

France built a tail-sitting jet with a ring for a wing. It killed its test pilot's career.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The SNECMA C.450 Coleoptere flew vertically in 1958, balanced on a single annular wing around a turbojet. After eight tethered hops, the ninth free flight pitched uncontrollably and pilot Auguste Morel ejected at low altitude. The program died with the prototype.
