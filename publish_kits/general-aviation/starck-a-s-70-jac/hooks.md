# Hooks for Starck A.S. 70 Jac

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A French homebuilt, single seat, post-war. Almost no one has heard of it. That's exactly why we're talking about it.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Andre Starck designed light airplanes the way some people write poetry: by himself, in France, in extraordinarily small batches. The AS-70 Jac is a single-seat light aircraft from the mid-1940s, one of dozens of obscure French postwar light singles that filled the void when the country's industry was rebuilding. In this video, the kind of airplane that only exists today because a handful of people refused to let it disappear.
