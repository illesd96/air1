# Hooks for Starck AS-57

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Side-by-side seating. Low wing. France. Just after WWII. Ten built.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Starck AS-57 is one of André Starck's small post-war light aircraft designs — a two-seat, side-by-side, low-wing single. Only ten were built. One was still flying as recently as 2012. In this video, the kind of micro-production French light airplane that almost defines the post-war European general aviation cottage industry.
