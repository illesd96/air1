# Hooks for Stinson 108

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The 1946 four-seater that came home from war and refused to retire.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Built by Stinson in the boom years right after WWII, the 108 carried families and bush pilots alike on a 165-hp Franklin. Fabric wings, steel tube fuselage, and a stance that still draws crowds at every taildragger gathering eighty years on.
