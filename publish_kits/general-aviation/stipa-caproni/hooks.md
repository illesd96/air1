# Hooks for Stipa-Caproni

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The 1932 Italian plane whose entire fuselage was a ducted fan.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Luigi Stipa believed the future of aviation was a barrel-shaped fuselage with the engine and propeller buried inside it: a ducted fan turned airframe. Caproni built it, flew it, and proved he was right about almost everything except practicality.
