# Hooks for Stoddard-Hamilton Glasair III

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A homebuilt two-seater that hits 270 knots on Lycoming power.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When Stoddard-Hamilton turned the Glasair II's retract sport plane into the Glasair III, they bolted on a 300-hp IO-540 and refined the composite airframe until it could outrun most certified singles. Builders in Arlington, Washington spent thousands of hours making one of the fastest piston homebuilts ever flown.
