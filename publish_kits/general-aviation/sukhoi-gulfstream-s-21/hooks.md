# Hooks for Sukhoi-Gulfstream S-21

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Russia and America almost built a supersonic business jet together in the 90s.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

After the Cold War ended, Sukhoi and Gulfstream teamed up on the S-21, a Mach 2 business jet that would have carried executives across continents in hours. Funding, politics, and economics killed it before metal was cut, but the design work mapped the trade space modern supersonic startups are still wrestling with.
