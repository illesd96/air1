# Hooks for Tecnam P2002

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Italy's two-seat trainer that quietly took over European flight schools.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Designed in the early 2000s by the Pascale brothers in Capua, the P2002 Sierra paired a Rotax 912 with a low-wing all-metal airframe and an Italian eye for proportion. At one point it accounted for seventy percent of Tecnam's output. Schools love it. Owners keep it.
