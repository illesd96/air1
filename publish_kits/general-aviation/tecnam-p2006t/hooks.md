# Hooks for Tecnam P2006T

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The lightest certified twin in production. Burns mogas.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When EASA changed CS-23 to allow lighter twin-engine certification, a small family-owned Italian factory near Naples saw an opening. The Tecnam P2006T weighs 1,180 kilograms empty, runs on two Rotax 912 engines, sips automotive premium gasoline, and trains hundreds of multi-engine students every year across Europe and the US. It's the answer to the question 'why is the multi rating so expensive' — and it's deeply underrated.
