The Tecnam P2006T is an Italian-built, shoulder-winged, all-metal light twin that received EASA CS-23 certification in 2009 and FAA Part 23 certification in 2010. Two Rotax 912 S3 engines, four seats, retractable gear, and a max takeoff weight under 1,250 kg.

In this video: how Costruzioni Aeronautiche Tecnam — a family business based in Capua, near Naples — found a niche between LSAs and the Diamond DA42, why flight schools love the operating cost of a Rotax twin, the honest engine-out story when you're already small and light, and what the P2006T actually delivers as a personal traveling airplane.
