# Hooks for Tecnam P2008

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Italian composites, American flight school. Underrated trainer.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When Tecnam introduced the P2008 at AERO Friedrichshafen in 2009, it was the company's first major composite project — a high-wing, two-seat single aimed squarely at the US LSA market. Today, fifteen years later, the P2008 is quietly dominating new-build LSA orders at flight schools that got tired of paying to maintain 1970s 152s. Here's why the P2008 deserves a closer look from the next generation of student pilots.
