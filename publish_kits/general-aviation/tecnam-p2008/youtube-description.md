The Tecnam P2008 is a single-engine, high-wing, two-seat Light Sport Aircraft built by Costruzioni Aeronautiche Tecnam in Capua, Italy. Introduced at AERO Friedrichshafen in April 2009 with first deliveries in December 2009, it was the first Tecnam to incorporate major composite components — composite fuselage shell, aluminum wings — combining low operating cost with modern build quality.

In this video: how the P2008 fits into the LSA category in the US and the ULM category in Europe, the Rotax 912 ULS economics that have made the P2008 the new go-to trainer for cost-conscious flight schools, the avionics options (Garmin G3X integration), and an honest comparison against the Cessna 152, Skycatcher and Czech competition.
