# Hooks for The Airplane Factory Sling TSi

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A South African four-seater kit that has flown around the world.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Sling Aircraft built a four-place evolution of the Sling 4, mated a Rotax 915iS turbo to it, and the TSi was born. Cruise the high 130s of knots on autogas, fly across oceans, finish a kit at home. The Johannesburg-based company has quietly become one of the most respected names in homebuilding.
