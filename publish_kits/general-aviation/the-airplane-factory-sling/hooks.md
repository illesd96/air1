# Hooks for The Airplane Factory Sling

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A South African two-seater that flew around the world in 40 days.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Mike Blyth and James Pitman built the Sling 2 in Johannesburg, then flew the second prototype around the world westbound in 40 days in 2009: the first time any aircraft of its class had circumnavigated. It made the company, and the LSA market took notice.
