# Hooks for Titan T-51 Mustang

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A three-quarter scale P-51. You can build it in your garage.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

John Williams of Titan Aircraft did something brave: he scaled down North American's most iconic warbird to about three-quarters size, swapped the Merlin V-12 for a Suzuki-based H6 or a Honda V8, and sold it as a kit. The Titan T-51 Mustang is the most credible warbird replica in homebuilt aviation — and it fits in an EAA chapter hangar. For the price of a new Cirrus you can have a tail-dragging, bubble-canopy fighter you built yourself.
