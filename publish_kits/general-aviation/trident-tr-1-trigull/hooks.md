# Hooks for Trident TR-1 Trigull

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Canadian amphibian that built three prototypes and went bankrupt.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Trident Aircraft of Burnaby, then Sidney, British Columbia built three prototypes of the TR-1 Trigull in the 1970s: a five-seat amphibious flying boat aimed at lake-country flying. The certification effort drained the cash, no buyers stepped up, and the company folded. The Trigull became a quiet what-if of Canadian aviation.
