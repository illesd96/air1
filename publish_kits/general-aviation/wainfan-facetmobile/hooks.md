# Hooks for Wainfan Facetmobile

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The faceted lifting-body homebuilt drawn by a Northrop aerodynamicist.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Barnaby Wainfan is a Northrop Grumman aerodynamicist and Kitplanes columnist. In 1993 he built and flew his FMX-4 Facetmobile, a faceted low-aspect-ratio lifting body that looks like an F-117 had a baby with a Volkswagen. It flies remarkably well and is one of the most original homebuilts ever drawn.
