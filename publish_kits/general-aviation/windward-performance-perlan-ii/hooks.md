# Hooks for Windward Performance Perlan II

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The pressurized glider that climbed to 76,000 feet on no engine at all.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Greg Cole designed the Perlan 2 for one mission: ride the polar stratospheric mountain waves to the edge of space. Pressurized cabin, sixty-foot composite wings, no engine. On 2 September 2018 it climbed to 76,124 feet over Patagonia, the highest a wing-borne aircraft has ever flown without propulsion.
