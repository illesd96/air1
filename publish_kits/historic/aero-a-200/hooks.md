# Hooks for Aero A.200

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Czechoslovakia built a four-seat sport plane specifically to win a 1934 European championship.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Challenge 1934 was the European touring plane championships — a serious international competition that swept dozens of light aircraft across the continent on a multi-stage rally. Czechoslovakia wanted to win. So Aero Vodochody built an aircraft purely for the contest: the A.200, a low-wing four-seat monoplane designed around the rule book. It came close. Here's the story of an aircraft built to beat a single specification, in a single year.
