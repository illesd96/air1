# Hooks for ANBO III

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Lithuania, 1929. A country that hadn't existed for 100 years built its first series-produced aircraft. Eight were made.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When Lithuania re-emerged as an independent state after 1918, it had no aircraft industry. So a Lithuanian army officer named Antanas Gustaitis began designing aircraft himself — and the ANBO III, in 1929, became the first Lithuanian design ever built in series.
