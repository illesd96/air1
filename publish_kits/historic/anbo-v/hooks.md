# Hooks for ANBO V

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Lithuania built its own trainer in 1931 — and almost no one remembers it existed.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1931, a tiny Baltic state with barely an aviation industry rolled out a parasol-wing trainer designed by an engineer whose initials gave the aircraft its name. The ANBO V wasn't fast, wasn't famous, and didn't change the war — because by the time war came, Lithuania had been swallowed twice. This is the story of an aircraft program a country built from nothing, and that history almost completely forgot.
