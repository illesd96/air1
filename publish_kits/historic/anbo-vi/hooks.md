# Hooks for ANBO VI

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Lithuania's second indigenous trainer fixed everything that was wrong with the first — then got erased by occupation.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Two years after the ANBO V, Antanas Gustaitis went back to his drawing board. The ANBO VI of 1933 took the same parasol-wing layout, bolted in a more powerful engine, redesigned the landing gear, and tried to push Lithuania's tiny aircraft industry one step closer to military self-sufficiency. It was a serious airplane. And it was almost completely wiped from collective memory by 1940. This is the story of the trainer that refined Lithuania's national aviation program just before its country disappeared.
