# Hooks for ANBO VIII

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Lithuania designed and built its own bomber in 1939. It first flew six days after Germany invaded Poland, and never saw combat.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

On September 5, 1939, while Stukas were already over Warsaw, Lithuanian engineer Antanas Gustaitis first flew the ANBO VIII, his country's homegrown attack bomber. Eleven months later, the Soviet Union annexed Lithuania, Gustaitis was executed in 1941, and the only prototype was destroyed. This is the bomber of a vanished air force.
