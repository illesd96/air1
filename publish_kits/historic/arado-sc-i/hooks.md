# Hooks for Arado SC I

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

In 1926 a German firm built a trainer biplane intended for a secret military flying school inside the Soviet Union. It wasn't accepted.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Behind the official disarmament of Versailles, Germany and the Soviet Union ran a secret military aviation school at Lipetsk, deep inside Russia. The Arado SC I was designed for it. The Reichswehr turned it down — but the company that built it would later make some of the Luftwaffe's most important aircraft.
