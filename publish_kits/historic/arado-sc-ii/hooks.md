# Hooks for Arado SC II

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Arado SC I was rejected. So Arado went away and built the SC II — and that one actually went into production.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1928, Arado had just been turned down by the secret Lipetsk flying school in Russia. So the company adjusted the design — a bigger engine, refined structure — and produced the SC II. This time, the Deutsche Verkehrsfliegerschule placed an order. The Luftwaffe was being built in plain sight.
