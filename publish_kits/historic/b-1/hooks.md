# Hooks for B-1

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Long before the supersonic B-1 Lancer, Boeing's first commercial aircraft was a 1919 wooden flying boat carrying mail between Seattle and Victoria. The very first 'B-1.'

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Yes, there was a Boeing B-1 before the Lancer. Built in 1919 by William Boeing himself, it was a wooden biplane flying boat that flew the first international airmail contract in U.S. history. This is the original B-1, and the airmail route that kept the Boeing Company alive.
