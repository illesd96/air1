# Hooks for North American B-25 Mitchell

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Sixteen medium bombers. One aircraft carrier. One way to Tokyo.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

On April 18, 1942, sixteen B-25 Mitchells took off from the deck of the USS Hornet. They had less than half the runway a B-25 was supposed to need. None of them had ever taken off from a carrier before. None of them would land on one. They flew over 600 miles to bomb Tokyo - and for the rest of their lives, those crews were known by one name.
