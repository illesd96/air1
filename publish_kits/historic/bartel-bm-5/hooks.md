# Hooks for Bartel BM-5

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Almost every pilot of the legendary Polish 303 Squadron — the highest-scoring RAF squadron of the Battle of Britain — learned to fly on this biplane.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Poland's interwar military pilots needed an advanced trainer. So Ryszard Bartel — a Pole who had trained at the German Albatros works during WWI — designed the BM-5. By 1939 the entire fleet was gone. By 1940, the pilots it had trained were flying Hurricanes over Britain.
