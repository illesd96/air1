# Hooks for Bernard 70

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Bernard 70 was a French fighter design that was never built. It was redrawn into a racing monoplane, then redrawn again into a fighter, and only two of THAT version were ever built. The Bernard 70 itself never existed except on paper.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Société des Avions Bernard sketched out a single-seat monoplane fighter in 1930, the Bernard 70. They never built it. They developed it into the Bernard S-72 racer, then back into the Bernard 74-01 and 74-02 fighters, both of which made it to two prototypes. This is the aircraft whose lineage outlived its own existence.
