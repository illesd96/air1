# Hooks for Blackburn First Monoplane

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Robert Blackburn's very first aircraft crashed on its first attempted flight in 1910. He never let anyone fly it again. Today the founder of one of Britain's great aircraft companies is remembered for an aircraft that essentially never flew.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1909, Robert Blackburn built a parasol monoplane in Leeds, copying what he'd seen in France. He hired pilot Robert Weiss to attempt the first flight in 1910. It crashed almost immediately. Blackburn shelved it and moved on. The 'Blackburn First Monoplane' became the foundation of one of Britain's biggest interwar aircraft firms. This is the failure that built an empire.
