# Hooks for Boeing 247

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The first modern airliner. Beaten by the DC-3 in two years.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When the Boeing 247 first flew in 1933, it made every existing airliner obsolete overnight. Retractable gear. All-metal monocoque construction. Trim tabs. Two cowled radials. It was the airliner of the future - and Boeing made the strategic error of refusing to sell one to a TWA executive named Jack Frye. So Frye called Douglas. The DC-1 followed. Then the DC-3. Boeing's lead in airliners ended before it began.
