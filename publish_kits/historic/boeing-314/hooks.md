# Hooks for Boeing 314 Clipper

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A flying hotel. The first plane to fly an American president overseas. Pan Am's lost masterpiece.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Before runways could handle large transoceanic aircraft, you built your airliner to land on water. The Boeing 314 Clipper was a flying ocean liner - sleeper cabins, dining room, lounge, separate dressing rooms for men and women. Pan American Airways flew them from San Francisco to Hong Kong and from New York to Lisbon. Only twelve were ever built. None survive.
