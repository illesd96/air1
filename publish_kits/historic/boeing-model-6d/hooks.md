# Hooks for Boeing Model 6D

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

In 1928, Boeing built a pusher biplane flying boat that nobody wanted. Only a handful were made. It is one of the most obscure aircraft in Boeing's catalog.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Boeing Model 6D is the aircraft Boeing pretends it never built. A pusher biplane flying boat developed in 1928, with confusing nomenclature spanning Model 6D, 6E, B-1D, and B-1E, almost nobody bought one. This is the forgotten corner of Boeing's history before the 247 and the Stratoliner.
