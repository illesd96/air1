# Hooks for Boeing Model 7

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Boeing's Model 7, also called the BB-1, was a 1920 flying boat where the pilot sat in front and the two passengers crammed in directly behind him. It was Boeing's quiet pivot away from the military.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Right after WWI, Boeing built a tiny biplane flying boat called the Model 7 or BB-1, with a cockpit so tight the two passengers sat shoulder-to-shoulder behind a single pilot. It almost no one bought. But it set the template for Boeing's interwar civil work. Here's the forgotten link between the original B-1 and the Boeing of today.
