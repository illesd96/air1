# Hooks for CAMS 37

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

France kept this biplane flying boat in front-line service from 1926 all the way into WWII — long after every other navy had moved on.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The CAMS 37 first flew in 1926. By 1939, virtually every other navy had retired its biplane flying boats. But the French were still using CAMS 37s for coastal patrol, mail, and training. This is the story of an aircraft that wouldn't quit.
