# Hooks for CANT 18

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Italy in the 1920s decided every flying-boat pilot needed to learn on a smaller flying boat first — and built this little biplane to do it.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Trieste in the 1920s was the centre of Italian flying boat manufacturing, and CANT was building airliners for trans-Adriatic routes. To train the pilots who would fly them, CANT built a miniature version: the CANT 18. A flying boat to learn flying boats on.
