# Hooks for Caudron C.600 Aiglon

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Before Saint-Exupéry was famous for The Little Prince, French pilots like him were touring Europe in little wooden monoplanes like this one.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1935, French aviation was at a strange peak — beaten in records by the Americans, anxious about Germany, and somehow still building elegant little touring monoplanes for a fading interwar leisure class. The Caudron C.600 Aiglon is one of the most graceful and least remembered of them.
