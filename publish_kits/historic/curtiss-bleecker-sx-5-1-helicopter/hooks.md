# Hooks for Curtiss-Bleecker SX-5-1 Helicopter

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

In 1926, an engineer named Bleecker built a helicopter with propellers mounted on each rotor blade, driven through gears from a single central engine. It vibrated itself to death. It is one of the most bizarre rotary-wing aircraft ever built.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Maitland Bleecker thought you could solve the helicopter problem by putting a propeller on each rotor blade. The Curtiss-Bleecker SX-5-1 was the result: a giant rotary-wing contraption with shafts running through each blade to drive its own little propeller. It vibrated terribly, never made a sustained free flight, and was scrapped. This is one of the strangest aircraft ever built.
