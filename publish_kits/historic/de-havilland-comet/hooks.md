# Hooks for de Havilland Comet

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The world's first jetliner. It broke apart in the sky. Square windows.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1952 the de Havilland Comet 1 entered service with BOAC. The jet age in commercial aviation began. Two years later, three Comets disintegrated in mid-air. The cause - metal fatigue around square cabin windows - would rewrite aircraft design forever, hand the jet airliner market to Boeing, and make the Comet a case study in every engineering school in the world.
