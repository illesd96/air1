# Hooks for de Havilland Mosquito

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Made of wood. Faster than the Spitfire. The Luftwaffe couldn't catch it.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When de Havilland proposed an unarmed bomber made of wood in 1938, the Air Ministry laughed them out of the room. Three years later, the Mosquito was the fastest operational aircraft in the world - and an unarmed wooden bomber that nothing the Luftwaffe could put in the air could intercept. They called it the Wooden Wonder. The Germans called it the Mossquito - and meant it.
