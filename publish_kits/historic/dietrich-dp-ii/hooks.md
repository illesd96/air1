# Hooks for Dietrich DP.II

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

After WWI, Germany was banned from building military aircraft — so designers like Richard Dietrich built 'training biplanes' that were nothing of the kind.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

1923 Germany was in the depths of the Weimar inflation, banned by Versailles from military aviation, and quietly rebuilding aircraft industry under civilian cover. The Dietrich DP.II Bussard — designed at the Dietrich-Gobiet Flugzeugwerke in Kassel — is one of dozens of 'sport biplanes' that fit that pattern.
