# Hooks for Douglas DC-3 (historic context)

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The airliner that won the war. And the war that nearly killed the airliner.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In civilian dress it was the DC-3, the plane that made commercial aviation profitable for the first time. In olive drab it was the C-47 Skytrain - the workhorse Eisenhower listed alongside the bazooka and the jeep as one of the weapons that won the Second World War. Most DC-3s flying in 2026 were built between 1940 and 1945.
