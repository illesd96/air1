# Hooks for Flyer I

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

December 17, 1903: 12 seconds, 120 feet, four flights. The story you think you know about the Wright Flyer is mostly wrong.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Everyone knows the Wright Flyer made the first powered flight on December 17, 1903. Almost everything else most people 'know' about it is partly wrong — including how long it stayed flyable, what happened to the airframe, and how long it took the world to actually believe the Wrights had done it.
