# Hooks for FMA I.Ae. 27 Pulqui I

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Two years after WWII, Argentina built the first jet fighter in Latin America, using French engineers and a captured Rolls-Royce engine. It was awful.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1947, Argentina, fueled by Peronist ambition and a recruited team of French wartime aviation engineers, flew the Pulqui I. It was the first jet fighter designed and built in Latin America. It also handled badly, vibrated alarmingly, and was scrapped after a single prototype. This is how Argentina almost became a jet power.
