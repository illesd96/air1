# Hooks for Focke-Achgelis Fa 330

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

German U-boats towed a one-man rotor kite into the sky at the end of a steel cable to spot ships.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

It is not a helicopter. It is not exactly a glider. The Focke-Achgelis Fa 330 Bachstelze — Wagtail — was an unpowered rotor kite that German Type IXD2 U-boats towed into the air on a 150-metre cable to give the lookout an extra 100 metres of altitude. About 200 were built. The pilot was a sailor with a few hours of training. The system was used in the Indian Ocean. Here's how it actually worked.
