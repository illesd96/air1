# Hooks for Focke-Wulf Fw 190

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

For nine months in 1941, no Allied fighter could touch it. The RAF called it a shock.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When the Focke-Wulf 190 first appeared over the Channel in August 1941, RAF pilots assumed they were seeing captured American Curtiss Hawks - it didn't look German. It outflew, outclimbed, and outdove the Spitfire Mark V. The Air Ministry's response was panicked enough that Britain seriously considered a commando raid to steal one.
