# Hooks for Focke-Wulf GL 18

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Focke-Wulf's earliest airliner barely flew, barely sold, and helped shape what the company became.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Before the Fw 190. Before the Condor. Before Kurt Tank. Focke-Wulf was a small German firm trying to build civil transports, and in 1926 it rolled out the GL 18 — a small light transport monoplane that almost no one bought. The history of Focke-Wulf does not begin with fighters. It begins with airliners that struggled. The GL 18 is a fingerprint of that early commercial period.
