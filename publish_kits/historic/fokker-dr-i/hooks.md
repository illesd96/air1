# Hooks for Fokker Dr.I

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Red Baron flew the Dr.I for six months. He scored most of his kills in something else entirely.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Fokker Triplane is the most famous fighter of the First World War. It is also one of the least successful designs Manfred von Richthofen ever flew. He scored 80 victories total - only 19 of them in a Dr.I. Yet because of how he died, this aircraft became the icon of an entire war.
