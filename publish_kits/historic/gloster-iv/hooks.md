# Hooks for Gloster IV

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Before the Spitfire there was a tiny biplane racer that taught Britain how to win the Schneider Trophy.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

1927. The Schneider Trophy is the most prestigious speed contest in the world, and Britain has decided it wants to win. Gloster's answer is a single-engined biplane floatplane — outwardly conservative compared to its rivals — that became one of the fastest biplane floatplanes ever built. The Gloster IV is the airplane that bridges Edwardian biplane construction and the monoplane revolution. Here's why it matters far more than its anonymity suggests.
