# Hooks for Heinkel HE 10

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Heinkel's earliest trainers were floatplanes — sold quietly to navies who wanted nothing official to do with Germany.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The He 111 came later. Before that there was Ernst Heinkel building floatplane trainers in the late 1920s, designs the company could legally export under the loose civil exception in the Versailles regime. The Heinkel HE 10 is one of those. It is the kind of aircraft on which the German export industry was rebuilt — quietly, abroad, and one foreign navy at a time.
