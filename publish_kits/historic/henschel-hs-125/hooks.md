# Hooks for Henschel Hs 125

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Henschel built two prototypes of an advanced trainer in 1934. The Luftwaffe rejected both. The designation 'Hs 125' is now famous, just not for this aircraft.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Long before Hawker Siddeley's HS-125 business jet, Henschel und Sohn AG built a low-wing, single-engine advanced trainer prototype called the Hs 125. The Luftwaffe tested two airframes in 1934 and chose the Arado Ar 96 instead. Henschel pivoted to building tanks. This is the trainer the Luftwaffe rejected, and the designation it lost to a British jet.
