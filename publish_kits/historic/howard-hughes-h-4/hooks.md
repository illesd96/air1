# Hooks for Hughes H-4 Hercules

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

It flew once. For one minute. And held the wingspan record until 2019.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Howard Hughes called it the Hercules. The press called it the Spruce Goose - and he hated the name. On November 2, 1947, with the world watching, a billionaire eccentric piloted a wooden flying boat the size of a city block exactly 70 feet above the water of Long Beach, California, for one mile. It would never fly again.
