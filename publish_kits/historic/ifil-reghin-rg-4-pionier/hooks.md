# Hooks for IFIL-Reghin RG-4 Pionier

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Communist Romania built exactly 50 of these primary gliders in a small factory in Transylvania — and then quietly stopped.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1954, Romania was still piecing together a Soviet-aligned aviation industry from the ruins of the Antonescu era. The IFIL-Reghin RG-4 Pionier was one of the very first Romanian-designed gliders of that new era — and almost nobody outside Romania has ever heard of it.
