# Hooks for Junkers Ju 52

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Corrugated metal. Three engines. And it carried half the German army into Crete.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When Lufthansa needed a reliable airliner in 1932, they bought the Junkers Ju 52. When the Wehrmacht needed paratroop transports for Crete in 1941, they used the same aircraft. By 1944 a third of all surviving Ju 52s had been shot down dropping supplies into Stalingrad. Tante Ju - Auntie Ju - was loved and lost in equal measure.
