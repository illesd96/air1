# Hooks for Junkers K 37

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Junkers sold a 'mail plane' to Sweden in 1927. Sweden quietly turned it into a bomber called the K 37, and shipped the prototype to imperial Japan. The Treaty of Versailles couldn't reach Stockholm.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Banned from building military aircraft by Versailles, Hugo Junkers set up a Swedish shell company called AB Flygindustri. They took the Junkers S 36 'mail plane' and built it as the K 37 bomber. One prototype eventually went to Japan, where it became the prototype of the Mitsubishi Ki-2. This is how Germany dodged Versailles.
