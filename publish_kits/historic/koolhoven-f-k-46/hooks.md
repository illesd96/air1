# Hooks for Koolhoven F.K.46

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Holland built its own training biplane in 1933 — and then in May 1940 the Luftwaffe destroyed almost the entire Dutch aircraft industry in four days.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Frederik Koolhoven was a Dutch engineer who had worked for Armstrong-Whitworth in Britain during WWI before going home to build aircraft for the Netherlands. The F.K.46 was one of his trainers. In May 1940, four days of fighting effectively ended Dutch military aviation.
