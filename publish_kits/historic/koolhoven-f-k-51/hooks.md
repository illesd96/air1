# Hooks for Koolhoven F.K.51

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Netherlands built its own basic trainer in 1935. The Germans captured them all in 1940 and used them to train Luftwaffe pilots for the rest of the war.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Frederik Koolhoven, the Dutch aviation pioneer who learned his trade in Britain, built the F.K.51 trainer in 1935. By 1940 the Wehrmacht had captured every Dutch military airfield, every F.K.51 with it. The Luftwaffe gladly used them to train its own pilots. This is the small biplane that served everyone.
