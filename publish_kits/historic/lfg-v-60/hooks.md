# Hooks for LFG V 60

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Weimar Germany was banned from building warplanes — so it built a trainer that quietly taught the next generation.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Five. That is how many LFG V 60 floatplane trainers were built in the mid-1920s. Five aircraft, in a country forbidden by treaty from rearming, designed by a company most people have never heard of. The Versailles restrictions reshaped German aviation into a culture of small, clever, dual-use airframes — and the V 60 is one of the most obscure survivors of that period. Let's look at what it actually did.
