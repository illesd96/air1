# Hooks for Lockheed AQM-60 Kingfisher

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Kelly Johnson designed a Mach 4 target drone so fast that Nike-Hercules missiles kept missing it, so the Pentagon canceled it for embarrassing the air defense network.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Before the SR-71, before the A-12, Kelly Johnson built a ramjet-powered target drone that out-performed the missiles meant to shoot it down. The AQM-60 Kingfisher humiliated America's Cold War air defenses so consistently that Congress quietly killed it. This is the obscure ancestor of the Blackbird almost no one talks about.
