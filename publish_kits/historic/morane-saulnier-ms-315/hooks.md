# Hooks for Morane-Saulnier MS.315

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

France trained a generation of pilots on this little 1932 monoplane — and then most of them flew into a war their country lost in six weeks.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When the Morane-Saulnier MS.315 first flew in 1932, France was still the world's leading aviation power on paper. By the time the last MS.315 retired from training duties in the 1960s, France had been defeated, occupied, and rebuilt — and the little high-wing monoplane had quietly trained pilots through all of it. This is the story of a primary trainer that outlived the air force that ordered it.
