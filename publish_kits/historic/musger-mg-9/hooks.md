# Hooks for Musger Mg 9

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

In 1936 a tandem two-seat Austrian glider broke world records — and then two years later Austria didn't exist as a country anymore.

Erwin Musger was a brilliant Austrian glider designer, and the Mg 9 was his peak: a tandem two-seater that set world and national records in 1936-37. Then the Anschluss came, Austria was absorbed into Germany, and Musger's career took a sharp turn.
