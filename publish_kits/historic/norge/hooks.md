# Hooks for Norge

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

1926: an Italian airship crossed the North Pole with a Norwegian explorer, an American sponsor, and an Italian designer. Two years later that team self-destructed.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Norge made the first verified flight to the North Pole on 12 May 1926, with Roald Amundsen, Umberto Nobile, and Lincoln Ellsworth aboard. It was an international triumph. By 1928, Amundsen and Nobile were enemies — and Amundsen would die trying to rescue Nobile from a polar crash.
