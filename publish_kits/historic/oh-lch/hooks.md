# Hooks for OH-LCH

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

There is only one airworthy Douglas DC-3 left in Finland. Its registration is OH-LCH. Locals call it 'Hotel.' It started life in 1942 as a U.S. Army Air Forces C-53.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

OH-LCH is not a model of aircraft. It's a specific airframe, a single Douglas DC-3 (originally C-53 Skytrooper), built in Santa Monica in 1942, that has been continuously airworthy in Finland for over 60 years. Today she is the only flying DC-3 in Finland. This is the story of one airplane and the volunteers who have kept her alive.
