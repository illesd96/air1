# Hooks for Piper PA-16 Clipper

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The plane that saved Piper Aircraft - by reusing every part the company had on the shelf.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

By 1947 Piper was bleeding money. The postwar boom in private aviation had not arrived. So William Piper's engineers did something every great American company has done in a crisis - they reused existing tooling. The PA-16 Clipper stretched the two-seat PA-15 Vagabond fuselage to four seats and sold for $2,295. Piper survived. Pan American sued over the name.
