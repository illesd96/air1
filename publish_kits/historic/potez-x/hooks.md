# Hooks for Potez X

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

France built a colonial transport in 1921 designed for landing strips that didn't exist yet.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The French colonial empire in 1921 was enormous, badly mapped, and connected mostly by ship. The Potez X was the answer: a general-purpose transport biplane intended for the kind of dirt strips, river clearings, and improvised airfields a colonial network actually had. It is the early grandparent of the bush plane. Here's the story of an aircraft that helped airline routes get drawn into territories that had never seen a runway.
