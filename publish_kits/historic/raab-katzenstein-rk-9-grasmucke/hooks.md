# Hooks for Raab-Katzenstein RK.9 Grasmücke

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

1920s Germany had aviation companies that built aircraft for sport, advertising, AND training — all at the same time. This is one.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Raab-Katzenstein RK.9 Grasmücke — Hedge-Sparrow — is a perfect example of how Weimar Germany kept its aviation industry alive. Sport, advertising, training: the same aircraft did all three. About twenty were built. Almost nobody remembers.
