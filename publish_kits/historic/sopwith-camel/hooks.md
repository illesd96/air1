# Hooks for Sopwith Camel

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

1,294 kills. More than any Allied fighter of WWI. Most of them flown by teenagers.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Sopwith Camel killed more enemy aircraft than any other Allied fighter of the First World War. It also killed more of its own pilots in training than the Germans ever did. The combination of a rotary engine, narrow wings, and a fierce right-turn gyroscopic torque turned the Camel into something a beginner could not survive - and something an expert could win the war with.
