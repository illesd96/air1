# Hooks for Spirit of St. Louis

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

33.5 hours alone. No radio. No forward window. One sandwich.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

On May 20, 1927, a 25-year-old airmail pilot took off from Roosevelt Field, New York, in an aircraft built in 60 days by a struggling San Diego startup. He could not see straight ahead. He had five sandwiches and a quart of water. Thirty-three and a half hours later, he landed in Paris and became, for one decade, the most famous man on Earth.
