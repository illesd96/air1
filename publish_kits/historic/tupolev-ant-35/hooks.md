# Hooks for Tupolev ANT-35

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Stalin's USSR built its own DC-3 competitor in 1936 and it cruised at 376 km/h, faster than the DC-3, but only nine were ever made.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Tupolev's ANT-35 was the Soviet answer to the DC-3. It was faster, sleeker, all-metal, and presented at the 1936 Paris Air Show to applause. Then Aeroflot decided they wanted the DC-3 instead. The ANT-35 disappeared into Soviet obscurity by 1944. Here's the airliner that almost defined Soviet civil aviation.
