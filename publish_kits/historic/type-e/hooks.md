# Hooks for Type E

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Before the Avro 504 became the most-produced trainer of WWI, there was the Type E — and Alliott Verdon Roe called it his first real success.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In March 1912, a small British company test-flew a tractor biplane that its designer would call his first 'truly successful' aircraft. The Avro Type E, later Type 500, was the direct ancestor of the legendary Avro 504. Without it, the history of British aviation looks very different.
