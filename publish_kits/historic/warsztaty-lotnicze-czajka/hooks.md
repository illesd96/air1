# Hooks for Warsztaty Lotnicze Czajka

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Poland built 160 of these little training gliders before WWII — and then in 1939 the Luftwaffe destroyed almost every one of them on the ground.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Between 1931 and 1939, a Warsaw glider workshop quietly turned out the Czajka — Lapwing — and it became the aircraft that taught a generation of Polish pilots to fly. Then came September 1939, and the entire fleet, like the country, was effectively wiped out in weeks.
