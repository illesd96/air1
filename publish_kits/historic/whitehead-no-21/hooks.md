# Hooks for Whitehead No. 21

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Gustave Whitehead claimed he flew a powered aircraft in Bridgeport, Connecticut on August 14, 1901, two years before the Wright brothers. Historians say no. The state of Connecticut says yes.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Did Gustave Whitehead beat the Wright brothers by two years? Newspapers in 1901 said so. Aviation historians spent a century saying no. The state of Connecticut passed a 2013 law saying yes. This is the most disputed claim in aviation history, and the bat-winged monoplane at the center of it.
