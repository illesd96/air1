# Hooks for Wright Flyer

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Twelve seconds. One hundred and twenty feet. Everything we know about flying started here.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

On a cold December morning in 1903, on a windswept dune in North Carolina, two bicycle mechanics from Ohio did something the world's wealthiest engineers had failed at for a century. The flight lasted twelve seconds. Five people saw it happen. And for almost five years, nobody believed them.
