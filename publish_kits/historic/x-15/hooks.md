# Hooks for North American X-15

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Mach 6.7. The edge of space. And the pilot was Neil Armstrong before he was famous.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Before Neil Armstrong walked on the moon, he flew the X-15 seven times. Before the Space Shuttle had a name, this rocket plane had already proven a winged spacecraft could survive reentry. The X-15 still holds the speed record for a manned aircraft - set in 1967 and untouched for almost sixty years.
