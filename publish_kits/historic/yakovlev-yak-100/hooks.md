# Hooks for Yakovlev Yak-100

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Yakovlev built its second-ever helicopter in 1949 — and lost the production contract to Mil.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Most people know Yakovlev for fighters and trainers. Few know that the bureau spent the late 1940s seriously chasing a Soviet single-rotor helicopter contract. The Yak-100 of 1948-1949 was their second attempt, a single-engine transport helicopter that flew well, met requirements, and still lost out to Mikhail Mil's Mi-1. Here's why Yakovlev never became a helicopter manufacturer.
