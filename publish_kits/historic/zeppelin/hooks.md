# Hooks for Graf Zeppelin LZ 127

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Round the world in 21 days. In 1929. In an airship.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Long before Hindenburg, there was Graf Zeppelin. LZ 127. In August 1929, this airship flew around the world - Friedrichshafen to Tokyo to Los Angeles to Lakehurst and home - in 21 days, with passengers, in luxury. It made 590 flights, carried 34,000 people, and never had a fatal accident. Then the Hindenburg burned, and the entire airship era ended in a single sentence.
