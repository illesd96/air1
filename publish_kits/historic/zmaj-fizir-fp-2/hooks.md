# Hooks for Zmaj Fizir FP-2

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Yugoslavia built this biplane in Zemun in 1933 and somehow kept it flying until 1947 — through Nazi occupation, civil war, and a new communist state.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Rudolf Fizir was a Croatian-Yugoslav aircraft designer working at the Zmaj factory in Zemun in the 1930s. His FP-2 trainer first flew in 1933 — and somehow survived everything the next 14 years threw at the Balkans.
