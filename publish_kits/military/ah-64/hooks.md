# Hooks for Boeing AH-64 Apache

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Sixteen Hellfires. A 30mm chain gun slaved to the pilot's eyes. The AH-64 Apache is the gold standard attack helicopter — and it's been for 40 years.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When the U.S. Army needed a successor to the AH-1 Cobra after Vietnam, it ran the Advanced Attack Helicopter program. Hughes won with what became the AH-64 Apache: a tandem-cockpit gunship with a chain-driven 30mm cannon slaved to the pilot's helmet sight, sixteen Hellfire missile pylons, and armor that could shrug off 23mm rounds. It debuted in Operation Just Cause, came of age in Desert Storm, and 40 years later the Apache is still the benchmark every other attack helicopter is measured against.
