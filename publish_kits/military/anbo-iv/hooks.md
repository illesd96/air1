# Hooks for ANBO IV

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Lithuania built its own reconnaissance aircraft in the 1930s. It was genuinely good. Then the country was swallowed by larger neighbors.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The ANBO IV was a reconnaissance aircraft designed by Lithuanian engineer Antanas Gustaitis and produced domestically in the 1930s. By the standards of contemporary European reconnaissance types it was fast, climbed well, and was structurally modern. Then the Soviet Union absorbed Lithuania in 1940 and the Germans pushed through a year later, and the ANBO fleet was destroyed or scrapped almost without a trace.
