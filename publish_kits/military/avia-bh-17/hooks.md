# Hooks for Avia BH-17

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Czechoslovak fighter that promised much in trials and disappointed in service.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1924 the young Czechoslovak Air Force ordered twenty four Avia BH-17 biplane fighters off the back of strong trials results. Within months of squadron service the BH-17 was withdrawn for unreliability, and the type became one of the early lessons in why trials performance and operational performance are not the same.
