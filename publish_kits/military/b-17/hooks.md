# Hooks for Boeing B-17 Flying Fortress

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Three engines out. Tail half gone. The B-17 Flying Fortress was the bomber that came home from anything.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When the Boeing Model 299 rolled out in 1935, a reporter looked at the five gun positions and called it a 'flying fortress.' The name stuck. So did the legend. The B-17 dropped more bombs than any other Allied aircraft in World War II, absorbed punishment that should have killed it dozens of times over, and brought its crews home from missions that no other bomber would have survived. This is the story of the airplane that took everything Germany could throw at it.
