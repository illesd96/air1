# Hooks for Northrop Grumman B-2 Spirit

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Two billion dollars per airframe. Twenty built, then the line was shut. The B-2 Spirit is the most expensive aircraft ever to roll out of a hangar.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When Jack Northrop died in 1981, he believed his flying-wing dream had died with him. Eight years later, the Air Force unveiled the B-2 Spirit — and the old man's design was vindicated, at a cost no air force had ever paid before. Twenty-one airframes. Roughly two billion dollars each. A flying wing with no tail, designed to slip through Soviet radar to reach Moscow, fielded just in time for the Soviet Union to dissolve. This is the bomber that proved Jack Northrop right, and the program that proved stealth would never come cheap.
