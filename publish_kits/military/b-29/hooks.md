# Hooks for Boeing B-29 Superfortress

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Pressurized cabin. Computerized gunnery. And the only aircraft to ever drop a nuclear weapon in combat. The B-29 Superfortress changed history twice.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Boeing B-29 Superfortress was the most expensive weapons program of World War II — bigger even than the Manhattan Project. It was the first pressurized bomber, the first with a remote-controlled gun system, and the first to fly six thousand miles round-trip with a meaningful bomb load. In August 1945, two of them ended the war. This is the story of the bomber that defined strategic airpower for the next 80 years.
