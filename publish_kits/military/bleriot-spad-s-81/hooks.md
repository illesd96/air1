# Hooks for Blériot-SPAD S.81

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The 1923 French fighter that beat the Dewoitine D.1 by being less radical and more reliable.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1923 the French Air Force ran a fighter competition between the Bleriot SPAD S.81 and the Dewoitine D.1. The Dewoitine was the bolder, more modern design. The S.81 was the conservative biplane choice. Eighty S.81s were ordered, and the story of why conservative beat radical is one of the more honest lessons in interwar French aviation.
