# Hooks for Boeing F-47

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

It hasn't flown yet. The Air Force says it's already the next dominant fighter on Earth. Meet the Boeing F-47, the first US sixth-generation jet.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In March 2025, the US Air Force ended one of the most secretive fighter competitions in modern history and announced its winner: a Boeing-designed, sixth-generation air-superiority fighter called the F-47. We have seen no production airframe. No public photo. Just an artist's rendering and a promise. The Pentagon wants at least 185 of them, with a longer reach than the F-22, the ability to control its own pack of unmanned wingmen, and a price tag the Air Force has not yet publicly defended. This is everything we know — and everything we don't — about the jet meant to replace the Raptor.
