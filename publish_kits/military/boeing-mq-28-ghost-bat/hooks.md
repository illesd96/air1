# Hooks for Boeing MQ-28 Ghost Bat

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Australia is building a stealth drone designed to fly alongside crewed fighters and take the risks they can't.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Boeing MQ-28 Ghost Bat is a stealth unmanned combat aircraft developed by Boeing Australia for the Royal Australian Air Force. Previously known as the Airpower Teaming System, it is designed to fly as a loyal wingman alongside crewed fighters, sharing sensors, absorbing risk, and pushing the manned aircraft farther from danger. It first flew in February 2022, and it represents Australia's first domestically developed military combat aircraft in more than fifty years.
