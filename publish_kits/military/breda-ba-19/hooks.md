# Hooks for Breda Ba.19

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Italian aerobatic trainer that taught Regia Aeronautica pilots to fly upside down.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1928 Breda built an aircraft whose sole job was to teach pilots how to fly inverted. The Ba.19 was a single-seat aerobatic biplane trainer that became the standard advanced training aircraft for the Italian Regia Aeronautica and starred at airshows across Europe through the early 1930s.
