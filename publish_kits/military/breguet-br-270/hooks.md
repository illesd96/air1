# Hooks for Breguet Br.270

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

France's sesquiplane spy that bridged the gap between WWI biplanes and WWII monoplanes.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Bréguet 270 is the aircraft France used to admit, quietly, that the war for the skies had changed. A metal-skinned sesquiplane reconnaissance machine designed in the late 1920s, it replaced an entire generation of canvas-and-wire scouts and gave the Armee de l'Air its first taste of what modern observation work would demand.
