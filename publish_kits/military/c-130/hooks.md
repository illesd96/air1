# Hooks for Lockheed C-130 Hercules

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Gunship. Firefighter. Hurricane hunter. Spy plane. The C-130 Hercules has done every job the Pentagon could invent — for 70 years.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Lockheed's C-130 Hercules first flew in 1954. It is still in production. It has landed on aircraft carriers it had no business landing on, picked up Skyhook-rigged agents mid-flight, fought wildfires from Idaho to Australia, hunted hurricanes through 200-mph winds, and rained 105mm howitzer fire as the AC-130 gunship. This is how one turboprop transport became the most versatile aircraft in military history.
