The Boeing — originally McDonnell Douglas — C-17 Globemaster III is the bridge between strategic and tactical airlift. It can carry an M1 Abrams main battle tank intercontinentally and then land that tank on a 3,500-foot semi-prepared field. In this video we cover the C-X program origins, the cost-overrun crisis that nearly killed it, the externally blown flap system that gives it short-field performance, and the Kabul evacuation moments that defined the airframe in 2021.
