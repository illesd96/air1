# Hooks for Caproni Ca.135

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Italian bomber Italy refused to use but Peru and Hungary went to war with.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Italy designed the Caproni Ca.135 as a modern medium bomber in 1935. The Regia Aeronautica took one look, ordered a small batch, and then quietly tried to forget about it. Peru and Hungary, however, bought it, took it to war, and the Ca.135 ended up dropping bombs over Soviet territory while Italians ignored it.
