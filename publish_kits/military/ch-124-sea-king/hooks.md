# Hooks for CH-124 Sea King

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Canadian Sea King that flew off small frigates and changed how navies thought about anti-submarine helicopters.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Canada took the Sikorsky SH-3 Sea King, fitted it with a Beartrap haul-down system, and started flying anti-submarine helicopters off destroyer-sized escorts that no one thought could operate them. The CH-124 served for 55 years and quietly rewrote naval helicopter doctrine.
