# Hooks for Curtiss XBT2C

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Curtiss tried to save itself with one last carrier bomber. The Navy chose the Skyraider instead.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Curtiss XBT2C was a prototype two-seat, single-engine carrier dive and torpedo bomber developed at the end of the Second World War to meet a 1945 US Navy requirement combining dive bombing, torpedo attack, and reconnaissance into a single airframe. Derived from the SB2C Helldiver, it competed against the Douglas BTD and BT2D, and lost. The winner was the Douglas Skyraider. The Curtiss aviation business never recovered.
