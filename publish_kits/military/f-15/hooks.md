# Hooks for McDonnell Douglas F-15 Eagle

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

104 air-to-air kills. Zero losses. The F-15 Eagle has the cleanest dogfight record in modern military aviation.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

After Vietnam, the U.S. Air Force realized something painful: the F-4 Phantom wasn't built to dogfight. The answer was 'not a pound for air-to-ground' — a pure air-superiority fighter designed around one mission, killing other fighters. The F-15 Eagle entered service in 1976 and went on to compile a 104-to-zero air combat record across every air force that has ever flown it. Fifty years later, the Eagle is still in production. This is why.
