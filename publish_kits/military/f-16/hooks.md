# Hooks for General Dynamics F-16 Fighting Falcon

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

It was supposed to be a cheap day-fighter. Fifty years and 25 air forces later, the F-16 Viper is still the most-exported jet fighter on Earth.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In the 1970s, a small group of officers called the Fighter Mafia wanted a small, light, agile jet that could win in a knife fight. The Pentagon wanted something bigger. The Fighter Mafia won — and the result was the F-16. Fly-by-wire, bubble canopy, 9-G airframe. Half a century later it is still rolling off the line, still fighting wars, and still the benchmark every other multirole fighter is measured against.
