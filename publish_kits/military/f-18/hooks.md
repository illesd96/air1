# Hooks for Boeing F/A-18 Hornet

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

It lost a fighter competition in 1975. Then it became the backbone of the US Navy, the Blue Angels demo jet, and the star of two Top Gun movies.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1974, the YF-17 Cobra lost the US Air Force's lightweight fighter competition to the YF-16. It should have been the end of the design. Instead the Navy picked it up, redrew it for carrier landings, twin engines, and a Naval-Aviator workload, and turned it into the F/A-18 Hornet — the multirole jet that for forty years has been the heart of every American carrier air wing. This is the story of the consolation prize that became one of the most important fighters of the late 20th century.
