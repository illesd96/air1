# Hooks for Lockheed Martin F-22 Raptor

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The deadliest air-superiority fighter ever built. Only 187 made it to operational squadrons. Then the Pentagon shut the line down.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The F-22 Raptor was the Advanced Tactical Fighter — a stealth jet that could supercruise, thrust-vector, and kill at ranges no enemy could even detect it from. It was meant to replace the F-15 in the hundreds. Then in 2009, with the line still hot, Robert Gates killed production at 195 airframes. Today it is the most capable air-superiority fighter on Earth, and the U.S. cannot legally export a single one. This is the story of the jet we built too well — and too few of.
