# Hooks for Lockheed Martin F-35 Lightning II

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

One airframe. Three services. A trillion dollars. The F-35 is the most expensive weapon ever built — and it can hover.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

It started as a promise: one stealth fighter for the Air Force, the Marines, and the Navy — built on a single production line to save money. Two decades and over a trillion program dollars later, the F-35 Lightning II is the most controversial fighter in aviation history, and somehow also the most exported fifth-generation jet on Earth. This is how a program that almost killed itself became the spine of Western air power.
