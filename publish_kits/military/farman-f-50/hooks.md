# Hooks for Farman F.50

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The French night bomber that replaced an entire generation of pusher biplanes in 1918.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Late in 1918, France finally retired the Voisin pusher biplanes that had carried the night bombing campaign for years. The Farman F.50 was the aircraft that replaced them. Twin engines in tractor configuration, a deeper fuselage, and the capacity to carry a meaningful bomb load to German targets across the front.
