# Hooks for Fiat R.2

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The first aircraft ever sold under the Fiat name, born in the rubble of post-war Italy.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When the war ended in 1918, Italian aircraft maker SIA was absorbed into Fiat, and the first plane to wear the Fiat badge was not a fighter, not a record breaker, but a humble two-seat reconnaissance biplane called the R.2. It is the Roman ancestor of every Fiat aircraft that came after.
