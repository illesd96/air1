# Hooks for Hiro H4H

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Japanese naval arsenal flying boat that bridged biplane and monoplane patrol aviation.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Hiro Naval Arsenal built its own aircraft, and the H4H was its bridge from the era of biplane flying boats to the monoplane patrol aircraft that would dominate Imperial Japanese Navy operations in the Pacific. A 1931 monoplane flying boat, the H4H is one of the harder-to-find footnotes in early Japanese naval aviation.
