# Hooks for Ilyushin Il-28

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Soviet Union built six thousand of these. Most people in the West have never heard of it. Meet the Il-28 — the bomber that quietly armed half the world.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1948, the Soviet Union needed a tactical jet bomber and needed it fast. Sergei Ilyushin's bureau answered with the Il-28: a straight-winged, twin-engined, glass-nosed bomber that was simple enough to build by the thousands and rugged enough to operate from rough Eastern Bloc airfields. More than 6,000 were produced. It armed the Warsaw Pact, the Egyptian Air Force during Suez, Chinese forces over Vietnam, and North Korea long after every Western contemporary was scrapped. The Il-28 is the bomber the West never quite respected — and never quite got rid of.
