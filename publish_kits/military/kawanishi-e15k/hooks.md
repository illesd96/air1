# Hooks for Kawanishi E15K

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A reconnaissance floatplane fast enough to outrun fighters. On paper. In practice, the engine kept failing.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Kawanishi E15K Shiun was an Imperial Japanese Navy reconnaissance floatplane designed to be faster than the fighters that might try to intercept it. Its solution was extreme: a contra-rotating propeller, jettisonable floats, and a powerful engine that the Japanese aviation industry never quite managed to deliver reliably. Only a handful were built, none performed as advertised, and the program was a quiet disaster usually labelled Norm by Allied intelligence.
