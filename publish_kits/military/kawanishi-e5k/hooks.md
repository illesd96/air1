# Hooks for Kawanishi E5K

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Same airframe as the Yokosuka E5Y, different builder, almost zero documentation in English.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Kawanishi E5K was the Kawanishi-built version of the Yokosuka E5Y reconnaissance floatplane, produced under license for the Imperial Japanese Navy in the early 1930s. It is one of those parallel-production aircraft that exists almost entirely in Japanese-language sources, leaving English-speaking historians with a single sentence and a designation. It served, it scouted, it was replaced, and that is roughly the entire English-language record.
