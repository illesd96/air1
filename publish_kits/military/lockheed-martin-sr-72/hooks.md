# Hooks for Lockheed Martin SR-72

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Son of Blackbird that may or may not exist, depending on which Lockheed press release you read.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Since 2013 Lockheed Martin has talked publicly about the SR-72, a hypersonic intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance concept aircraft positioned as a successor to the SR-71 Blackbird. As of 2025 no confirmed flight tests have occurred, but the speculation around it has become one of the more durable hypersonic stories in aerospace.
