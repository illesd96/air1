# Hooks for Macchi M.7

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Italian flying boat that won the Schneider Trophy and then served as a frontline fighter.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Most Schneider Trophy winners were specialized racers that never flew in combat. The Macchi M.7 broke that pattern. A single-seat flying boat fighter from the early 1920s, the M.7bis variant won the 1921 Schneider Trophy at Venice, and then the standard M.7 went on to serve in Italian Navy fighter squadrons through the rest of the decade.
