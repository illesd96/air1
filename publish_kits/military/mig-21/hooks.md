# Hooks for Mikoyan-Gurevich MiG-21

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Sixty countries. Eleven thousand built. The most-produced supersonic jet in history is still flying — and it costs less than a sports car.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The MiG-21 Fishbed is the Soviet Union's gift to every air force on a budget. A tailed delta with a single Tumansky turbojet, a needle nose, and just enough fuel for a knife-fight close to home. Over 11,000 built. Sixty operators. Combat in every Cold War proxy war from Vietnam to the Middle East. Today, decades after the Soviet collapse, MiG-21s are still flying patrols, still scrambling, still cheap enough that small air forces have nothing better.
