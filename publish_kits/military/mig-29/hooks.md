# Hooks for Mikoyan MiG-29

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Helmet-cued missiles. Thrust-to-weight over one. The MiG-29 Fulcrum was the Soviet answer to the F-16 — and in a knife fight, it was terrifying.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When the Soviet Union saw the F-15 and F-16, it set two design bureaus to work. Sukhoi got the heavy: the Su-27. Mikoyan got the light: the MiG-29 Fulcrum. With a helmet-mounted sight, the R-73 missile that could shoot 45 degrees off-boresight, and a thrust-to-weight ratio over 1, the Fulcrum could win a close-in fight against anything the West fielded. Then the Berlin Wall came down, and East German MiG-29s flew west into NATO hands. The West got an education.
