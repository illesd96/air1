# Hooks for General Atomics MQ-9 Reaper

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

It can loiter for 24 hours, see in the dark, and launch Hellfires from 30,000 feet. The MQ-9 Reaper is the war on terror's signature weapon.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The General Atomics MQ-9 Reaper is the aircraft that defined how America fought from 2007 onward. Twenty-seven hours of endurance, a sensor turret that can read a license plate from 25,000 feet, and four hardpoints that carry Hellfire missiles and 500-pound bombs. The pilot sits in a trailer in Nevada. The aircraft flies in Iraq, Yemen, Afghanistan, the Sahel. This is how one unmanned aircraft rewrote the rules of targeted warfare — and the ethical debate that came with it.
