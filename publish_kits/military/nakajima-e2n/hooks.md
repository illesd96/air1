# Hooks for Nakajima E2N

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Japanese reconnaissance seaplane that taught the Imperial Navy to fly from cruisers.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Before the famous Aichi E13A Jake and the Mitsubishi F1M Pete, the Imperial Japanese Navy learned the discipline of catapult-launched reconnaissance with the Nakajima E2N. A two-seat sesquiplane floatplane from the late 1920s, it was the bridge between Japan's licensed Hansa-Brandenburg designs and its first indigenous naval reconnaissance fleet.
