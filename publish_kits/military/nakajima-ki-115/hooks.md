# Hooks for Nakajima Ki-115

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A single-purpose aircraft built for a mission no one should ever have been asked to fly.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Nakajima Ki-115 Tsurugi is one of the saddest entries in aviation history. Built in 1945 for one mission and one mission only, designed to be cheap, simple, and disposable, it is the aircraft Japan built when it had run out of everything except young pilots. Treating it honestly means treating it carefully.
