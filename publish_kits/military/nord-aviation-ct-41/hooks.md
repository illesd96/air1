# Hooks for Nord Aviation CT.41

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The French supersonic drone that helped interceptor crews learn to chase the next generation of bombers.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

By the late 1950s, training a fighter pilot to intercept a supersonic bomber was a problem without a clear answer. You could not exactly hand them a real B-58 to chase. Nord Aviation built the CT.41 Narwhal as the solution, a French supersonic target drone designed specifically for interception training, and it is one of the more elegant pieces of Cold War training infrastructure.
