# Hooks for Nord Noroit

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

France built a flying boat for sea rescue. The Navy ordered exactly zero.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

After the Second World War, the French Navy needed a maritime patrol and rescue flying boat, and Nord Aviation offered the 1400 Noroit. It first flew in January 1949, performed competently, and then the Navy chose someone else's design. Only a handful were built before the program faded, leaving the Noroit as a footnote in the long French love affair with flying boats that ended before jets made them obsolete.
