# Hooks for Northrop AQM-38

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Over 2,000 built. Most were destroyed on purpose. Meet the AQM-38 — the target drone the US Army shot down to learn missiles.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When the US Army and Navy needed to teach surface-to-air missile crews how to actually hit something, they needed something to shoot at — and something they were willing to lose. The Northrop AQM-38, built by Northrop's Radioplane Division, was that something. A small, expendable drone, jet- or rocket-powered depending on variant, launched off the back of a truck, flown into the line of fire of a SAM battery, and (usually) blown out of the sky. Over 2,000 left the Van Nuys factory. Their job was to die. This is the aircraft built to be killed.
