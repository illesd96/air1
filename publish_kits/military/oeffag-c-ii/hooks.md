# Hooks for Oeffag C.II

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

An Austro-Hungarian reconnaissance biplane built by the company that made Albatros fighters under license.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Most people who know Oeffag know it as the Austrian company that built better Albatros D.III fighters than the Germans did. Few know that Oeffag also designed its own reconnaissance aircraft. The C.II is a small slice of Austro-Hungarian aviation history that almost vanished with the empire that built it.
