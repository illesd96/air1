# Hooks for North American P-51 Mustang

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A British engine in an American airframe. Together they broke the Luftwaffe's back. This is the P-51 Mustang.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1940, the British asked North American Aviation to build them P-40s. North American said no — and offered to design a new fighter in 120 days. The result was the P-51 Mustang. Mate it to the Rolls-Royce Merlin engine, fill the fuselage with fuel, and suddenly American bombers had a fighter that could escort them all the way to Berlin and back. This is the airplane that broke daylight bombing wide open.
