# Hooks for Potez 29

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

A French civilian airliner that the military quietly converted into an ambulance, transport, and trainer.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Henry Potez designed the Potez 29 in 1927 as a five-passenger civil airliner, and then watched the French Air Force buy more of them than any airline ever did. The 29 became the ambulance, the colonial taxi, the navigator trainer, and the long-distance record breaker that you have never heard of.
