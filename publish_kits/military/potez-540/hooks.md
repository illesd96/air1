# Hooks for Potez 540

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Spain. Morocco. France. A long-bodied French bomber that was obsolete on the day it entered service — but kept being sent into combat anyway.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Potez 540 first flew in 1933, and the French Air Force placed an enormous order. By 1936 it was the most numerous bomber in French service. The trouble was, it had been overtaken almost overnight by modern monoplane fighters. Then the Spanish Civil War broke out, and the Republicans bought what France could spare. Then the colonies needed something. Then in 1940 the Germans came. The Potez 540 was obsolete and unsuited for war, and it was sent into war anyway. This is the story of how an air force flies the planes it has, not the planes it needs.
