# Hooks for Pützer Elster

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The first German-designed and built military aircraft after World War II.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Postwar Germany was forbidden from designing and building aircraft until 1955. When Alfons Putzer flew the Elster in 1959, it was one of the first all-German postwar designs, a humble light trainer and recreational sport aircraft that served the Luftwaffe and Marineflieger and quietly marked Germany's return to its own aviation industry.
