# Hooks for R.31

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The only operational WWII military aircraft entirely designed and built in Belgium.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When the German Army crossed the Belgian frontier in May 1940, the Belgian Air Force met them with a small fleet of mostly foreign-built aircraft. One type, however, was entirely Belgian. The Renard R.31 was a parasol-wing reconnaissance monoplane and the only WWII operational military aircraft fully designed and built in Belgium.
