# Hooks for RediGO

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The last military aircraft ever designed and produced in Finland.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Finland has a long history of designing and building its own military aircraft, from the Valmet Vihuri to the Valmet L-70 Vinka. The Redigo, first flown in 1985, was the end of that line. A turboprop military basic trainer and liaison aircraft, it is the last domestic Finnish military aircraft and a quiet marker of the end of an era.
