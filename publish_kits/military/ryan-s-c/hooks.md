# Hooks for Ryan S-C

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Ryan built a smart little three-seat civilian monoplane. The Army grabbed exactly one and pressed it into service.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Ryan S-C was a three-seat cabin monoplane built by Ryan Aeronautical in the late 1930s, aimed at the civilian sports tourer market. At least one was impressed into United States Army Air Forces service during the war as the L-10, used for light utility and liaison work. Ryan would later become famous for the X-13 and the Firebee target drones, but in the late 1930s they were still building elegant little touring planes like this one.
