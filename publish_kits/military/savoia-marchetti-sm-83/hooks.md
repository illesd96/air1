# Hooks for Savoia-Marchetti SM.83

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Italy took a famous bomber, stripped the guns, and tried to sell it as an airliner.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Savoia-Marchetti SM.83 was the civilian airliner version of the SM.79 Sparviero bomber, one of Italy's best-known military aircraft of the late 1930s. With three engines, a humpbacked profile, and surprising speed, the SM.83 carried passengers on Italian and South American routes before the war swallowed it. Only a handful were built, and the type vanished beneath the shadow of its more famous military sibling.
