# Hooks for Short Empire

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The British flying boat that carried mail and passengers across the Empire and then went to war.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Before the Short Sunderland defended Atlantic convoys, there was the Short Empire. A four-engined monoplane flying boat designed in 1936 to fly mail and passengers from Britain to Cape Town, Singapore, and Sydney, the Empire defined the golden age of long-haul commercial aviation. When war came, those same hulls went into uniform.
