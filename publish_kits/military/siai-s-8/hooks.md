# Hooks for SIAI S.8

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Italy's 1917 flying boat that taught a generation of naval pilots how to land on water.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

Before Italy built a navy of fighters, it had to teach pilots to trust a wooden hull on the Adriatic. The SIAI S.8 was that floating classroom. A two-seat reconnaissance flying boat from 1917, it lived a quiet life patrolling coastlines while the famous fighters got the headlines, and its hull design quietly shaped the boats that followed.
