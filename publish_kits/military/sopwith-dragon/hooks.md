# Hooks for Sopwith Dragon

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The Snipe won the war. The Dragon got the radial engine that didn't quite work. This is the Sopwith fighter you never heard of.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Sopwith Snipe was the British fighter that took the RAF into the final months of WWI and the early years of the RAF. The Dragon was its strange cousin — same basic airframe, but powered by an ABC Dragonfly radial engine that the British aero industry was convinced would change everything. The Dragonfly proved unreliable, prone to overheating, and ultimately a strategic embarrassment. The Sopwith Dragon flew, served briefly, and disappeared, taking the dream of a quick British radial revolution with it. This is the fighter that paid the price for an engine that did not work.
