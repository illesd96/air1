# Hooks for Lockheed SR-71 Blackbird

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

It outran every missile ever fired at it. Mach 3.3, eighty-five thousand feet — and not one Blackbird was ever shot down.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In 1964, Lockheed's Skunk Works rolled out an aircraft that leaked fuel on the ground and expanded inches in flight. It was 93% titanium, painted black to radiate heat, and built to fly faster than any bullet from any anti-aircraft gun on Earth. The SR-71 Blackbird flew for 34 years, was shot at over 4,000 times, and never lost a single airframe to enemy fire. This is how Kelly Johnson's team built an aircraft we still cannot replace.
