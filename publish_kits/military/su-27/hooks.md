# Hooks for Sukhoi Su-27

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Pugachev's Cobra. Thrust-to-weight over one. The Sukhoi Su-27 Flanker is the Soviet F-15 — and it might be the more maneuverable jet.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

When the Soviet Union saw the F-15 and F-16 enter American service, it didn't just build one fighter to counter them. It built a family. Sukhoi's heavyweight Su-27 Flanker — twin-engine, big wing, leading-edge root extensions feeding vortices over a blended fuselage — was the answer to the F-15. At Paris 1989, Viktor Pugachev pulled the Cobra: nose up past 110 degrees while flying level. The West realized the Soviets had been hiding something. The Flanker family is still in production today.
