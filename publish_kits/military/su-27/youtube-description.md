The Sukhoi Su-27 Flanker is the Soviet heavyweight 4th-generation fighter — the one Mikoyan's MiG-29 was the lightweight partner to. Big wing, blended fuselage, leading-edge root extensions, and AL-31F engines that gave it a thrust-to-weight ratio above 1. In this video we cover the TPFI program, why the early Flanker prototypes (T-10) failed and forced a complete redesign, the legendary Pugachev's Cobra debut at Paris 1989, and the modern Flanker family (Su-30, Su-33, Su-34, Su-35) still rolling out today.
