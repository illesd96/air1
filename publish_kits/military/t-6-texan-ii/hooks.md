# Hooks for T-6 Texan II

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The most important military aircraft most civilians have never heard of. Almost every American military pilot meets one.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The Beechcraft T-6 Texan II is a single-engine turboprop primary and basic trainer derived from the Pilatus PC-9. Built in the United States by what is now Textron Aviation, it replaced the Cessna T-37 Tweet and the Beechcraft T-34 Mentor and now trains the bulk of American military pilots before they move on to advanced jet or rotary platforms. It is the unglamorous backbone of how the United States makes pilots.
