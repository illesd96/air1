# Hooks for Temco T-35 Buckaroo

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The American low-cost trainer that the US Air Force did not want and only a few foreign countries bought.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

In the late 1940s Temco designed the T-35 Buckaroo as an extremely low-cost military and commercial trainer. The US Air Force ran the evaluation and chose the Beech T-34 Mentor instead. Temco scrambled for export orders to keep the line open, and the Buckaroo became a textbook example of what happens when the home market says no.
