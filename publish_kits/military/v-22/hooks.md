# Hooks for Bell Boeing V-22 Osprey

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

Helicopter for takeoff. Airplane for cruise. Thirty years of development, $36 billion, and the V-22 Osprey is finally — mostly — working.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

The V-22 Osprey took the V/STOL dream of a tilting rotor and turned it into a 30-year, multi-fatality development saga. Crashes in 1991, 1992, 2000, 2010, 2017, 2022, 2023, 2024. Grounding after grounding. And yet: it carries twice as far, twice as fast as any helicopter alive. Today, three branches of the U.S. military and the Japanese Self-Defense Force fly it. This is the story of the tiltrotor that almost killed itself before it ever worked.
