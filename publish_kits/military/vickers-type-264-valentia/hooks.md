# Hooks for Vickers Type 264 Valentia

## Shortform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts — 3 sec)

The 1934 British biplane bomber-transport that carried troops across deserts well into WWII.

## Longform (YouTube — 30 sec cold open)

By 1934 the biplane bomber-transport was already an anachronism, but the Royal Air Force was operating an empire from Kabul to Cape Town and it needed something rugged, simple, and slow. The Vickers Type 264 Valentia, a converted Victoria with a stronger engine and beefed up structure, did that work for over a decade.
