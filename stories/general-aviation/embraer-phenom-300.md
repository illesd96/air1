# embraer-phenom-300
## Hooks
- The Phenom 300 has been the world's best-selling light business jet every year since 2013, beating Cessna and Beechcraft in their own home market.
- It's certified for single-pilot operations, meaning a wealthy owner-pilot can legally fly themselves around on a transcontinental jet without hiring a captain.
- NetJets, the fractional ownership giant owned by Warren Buffett, ordered hundreds of Phenom 300s — the largest single private-jet order in history at the time of signing.
- Embraer designed the cabin with help from BMW Designworks, giving the interior the same kind of industrial design language as a luxury car.
- The Phenom 300 cruises at 453 knots and flies up to 45,000 feet, putting it above almost all commercial air traffic and turbulence.

## Seed paragraph

A Brazilian aerospace company decided it could out-design Cessna in Cessna's own backyard. They hired BMW Designworks to style the cabin, certified the airplane for single-pilot flight, and priced it just below the giants. The Phenom 300 has been the world's best-selling light jet every year for over a decade — built in São José dos Campos, sold in Wichita's backyard.
